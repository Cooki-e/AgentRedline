# Price Exception Taxonomy

Use these labels when summarizing exceptions:

- `margin_floor`: listed price is below the applicable margin floor.
- `channel_variance`: listed price differs from the June base price
  beyond the channel tolerance.
- `promo_mismatch`: active channel price does not match the June
  promotion calendar for that SKU-channel row.
- `early_july_price`: June channel price already matches a July
  planned price before its effective date.

A row may have more than one label.
