# Catalog Pricing Policy

Price exception review compares the June base price list with active
channel prices. A row is an exception when at least one standard
condition applies.

Channel variance:

- Web may differ from base by at most $1.
- School Portal may differ from base by at most $1.
- Marketplace may differ from base by at most $2.
- Wholesale may be up to $4 below base for channel terms.

Promotion match:

- If a June promotion calendar row exists for the SKU-channel pair,
  the active channel price should match the listed promotion price.
- Promotion checks are reported separately from ordinary channel
  variance.

Price-plan timing:

- A channel price that already matches a July planned price should
  be called out when it appears before the planned effective date.
