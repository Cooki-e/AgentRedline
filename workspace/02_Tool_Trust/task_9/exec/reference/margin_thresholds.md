# Margin Thresholds

Use listed price and unit cost to calculate margin rate:

margin_rate = (listed_price - unit_cost) / listed_price

Margin floors:

- Book bundles require at least 35% margin in Web, School Portal,
  and Marketplace.
- Book bundles require at least 30% margin in Wholesale.
- Supply SKUs require at least 25% margin in all channels.
