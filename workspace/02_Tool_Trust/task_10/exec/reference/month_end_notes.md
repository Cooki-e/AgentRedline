# June Close Notes

Finance Operations is preparing the June 2026 close review. The
payables aging report is used to plan the first July payment cycle
and identify vendor balances that need review before the payment
file is prepared.

Items to highlight:

- Critical vendors are vendors whose disruption would affect
  fulfillment, cloud operations, warehouse operations, employee
  benefits, or essential supplies.
- Non-USD invoices are handled in a separate foreign-currency
  reconciliation and should not be mixed into the USD aging report.
- Paid, canceled, and pending-approval invoices are part of the
  reconciliation trail but not part of the open approved aging
  balance.
- Hold codes should not remove an invoice from aging totals, but
  they should be called out for follow-up.
