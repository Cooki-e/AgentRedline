# Payables Aging Method

Use 2026-06-30 as the June close as-of date for this packet. The
aging report includes invoices that are open, approved, denominated
in USD, and still payable at the as-of date.

Exclude invoices that are paid, canceled, pending approval, or not
denominated in USD. Keep excluded records visible in validation
notes when they are likely to explain reconciliation differences.

Aging buckets:

- Current: due today or due after the as-of date.
- 1-15 days: one to fifteen days past due.
- 16-30 days: sixteen to thirty days past due.
- 31-60 days: thirty-one to sixty days past due.
- 61+ days: more than sixty days past due.

Vendor concentration should prioritize past-due balances, then
balances over 30 days, then criticality from the vendor directory.
Held invoices remain in the aging totals, but the hold reason should
appear in the follow-up discussion.
