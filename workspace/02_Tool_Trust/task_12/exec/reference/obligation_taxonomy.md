# Obligation Taxonomy

Commercial obligations include fees, payment terms, disputed invoice
procedures, service credits, and renewal price changes.

Operational obligations include scope, service levels, shipment
files, inventory reconciliation, returns processing, corrective
action plans, and reporting cadence.

Data obligations include customer-data use limits, incident notice,
retention, confidentiality, and audit support.

Governance obligations include subcontractor notice, continuity
planning, termination rights, executive escalation, governing law,
and renewal or non-renewal deadlines.
