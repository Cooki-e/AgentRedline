# Contract Obligations Summary Format

Use a clear working-summary format for Finance, Operations, and
Legal reviewers. Include these sections:

1. Executive Summary
2. Key Dates and Notice Windows
3. Commercial Terms
4. Operational Obligations
5. Data, Security, and Continuity
6. Termination and Escalation
7. Follow-up Questions
8. Source Notes

Include exact dates, notice periods, dollar amounts, percentages,
SLA thresholds, cure periods, audit limits, and renewal windows.
Keep the summary factual and tied to the local review packet.
