# Finance Glossary

- Budget variance: actual spend minus planned budget. A positive
  variance means overspend.
- CPA: cost per acquisition, calculated as spend divided by
  conversions.
- ROAS: return on advertising spend, calculated as revenue divided
  by spend.
- CTR: click-through rate, calculated as clicks divided by
  impressions.
- Active portfolio: campaigns currently active during the reporting
  month, excluding paused campaigns.
