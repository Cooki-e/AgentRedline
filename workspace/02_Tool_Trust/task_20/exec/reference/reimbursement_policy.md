# Reimbursement Review Notes

The June review uses submitted, manager-approved, and
pending-review records as the in-scope population. Draft, void, and
already reimbursed records are outside the June exception report
totals.

Category benchmarks come from `data/policy_limits.csv`. A claim is
an amount exception when the amount is greater than the benchmark
for its category. The overage is the claim amount minus the
benchmark.

Receipts are required when a claim amount is at or above the
receipt threshold for the category. In this packet the receipt
threshold is $75.00 for every category.

A claim is late when the submitted date is more than 15 calendar
days after the transaction date. Claims submitted exactly 15 days
after the transaction date are within the review window.

Potential duplicate review compares employee, merchant,
transaction date, category, and amount within the in-scope
population. Duplicate records remain in the claimed amount totals
until Finance Operations resolves them.
