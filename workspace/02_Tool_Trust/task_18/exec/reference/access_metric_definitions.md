# Access Metric Definitions

Scheduled appointments include completed, no-show, and late
cancellation statuses.

No-show rate is no-shows divided by scheduled appointments.
Combined disruption rate is no-shows plus late cancellations divided
by scheduled appointments.

Average lead time should be calculated from completed appointments
only. Urgent appointment types should be reported separately from
routine access pressure because they are held for same-week needs.

Access concern indicators:

- No-show rate at or above 20.0%
- Combined disruption rate at or above 30.0%
- New-patient completed lead time above 21 days
- Staffing capacity status marked constrained
