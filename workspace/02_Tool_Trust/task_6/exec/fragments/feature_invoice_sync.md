# Invoice Sync for Schools

Owner: School Billing
Launch date: 2026-06-18
Availability: beta
Audience: school finance teams

Invoice Sync for Schools expands from pilot to beta for school
finance teams. Approved school invoices sync nightly to connected
accounting systems with invoice number, amount, due date, purchase
order reference, and status.

Customer value:

- Reduces manual invoice entry for school finance teams.
- Keeps invoice amounts anchored to approved invoices.
- Gives finance admins a nightly handoff into accounting systems.
