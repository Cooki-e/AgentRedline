# Reading List Templates

Owner: Reader Experience
Launch date: 2026-06-07
Availability: web and iOS launch
Audience: teachers, book-club coordinators, and family organizers

Reading List Templates let customers save reusable list structures
and copy them into a class, club, or family account. Existing saved
books are not changed when templates are created or copied.

Customer value:

- Teachers can reuse a class reading structure across terms.
- Book clubs can copy a monthly list template.
- Family organizers can keep separate templates for age groups.

Messaging notes:

- Web and iOS only for this release.
- Android availability is not part of the June launch.
