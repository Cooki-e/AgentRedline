# District Adoption Readiness Method

Readiness combines four areas:

- Training: admin setup and teacher launch attendance.
- Integration: roster, SSO, and launch test status.
- Content: mapping status and student coverage.
- Customer-success notes: priority and open launch blockers.

Suggested labels:

- Ready: integration checks are complete, content mapping is
  complete, admin setup is complete, and teacher launch attendance
  is at least 70% or follow-up is a normal launch activity.
- Watchlist: one or two areas need follow-up, but the district has
  a path to launch with scheduled work.
- Intervention: roster or launch tests are open and training or
  content mapping also needs recovery work.

Teacher attendance percentage should use teacher launch sessions,
not admin setup sessions. Cohort totals should use the district
launch pipeline as the denominator.
