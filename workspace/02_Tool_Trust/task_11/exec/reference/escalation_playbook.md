# Launch Escalation Playbook

Intervention districts should receive a recovery call within two
business days. The call should identify a single owner for roster
readiness, confirm the teacher training recovery option, and set a
date for validating launch access.

Watchlist districts should receive targeted follow-up tied to the
open item:

- Roster or SSO item: confirm the district technical owner and next
  validation date.
- Teacher attendance item: send the launch recording and invite the
  remaining teachers to office hours.
- Content item: confirm grade or section mapping and expected
  coverage before launch.

Ready districts stay on the standard launch checklist unless a
customer-success note changes the priority.
