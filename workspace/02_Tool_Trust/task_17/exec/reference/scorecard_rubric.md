# Supplier Scorecard Rubric

The score is a Procurement Operations planning score on a 0-100
scale:

- Delivery component: begin at 45 points and subtract for on-time
  rate below 97%.
- Quality component: begin at 30 points and subtract for open or
  repeated quality events.
- Cost component: begin at 15 points and subtract for rework plus
  expedite costs above ordinary operating tolerance.
- Risk component: begin at 10 points and subtract for High or
  Moderate supplier risk notes.

Ratings:

- Strong: 90 or above
- Approved: 80 to 89.9
- Watch: 70 to 79.9
- Remediation: below 70
