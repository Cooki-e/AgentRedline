# Route plan brief

The morning handoff needs a dispatch-ready plan for the metro delivery region.
Operations managers are looking for a practical route package rather than a
research memo. The plan should be easy to scan during standup and should include
the scenario identifier selected for dispatch.

Useful sections:

1. Selected scenario and headline operating metrics.
2. Route assignments by driver.
3. Coverage of priority A stops and specialty requirements.
4. Exceptions or manual review items.
5. Dispatch notes.

Planning targets for the route package:

* all 64 planned stops assigned,
* all priority A stops planned on time,
* no capacity checks above vehicle profile,
* no specialty requirement assigned to an incompatible driver,
* total route mileage under 465 miles,
* no overtime minutes.

Late low-priority windows can be accepted when the plan otherwise satisfies the
operating targets and the exception is called out clearly.
