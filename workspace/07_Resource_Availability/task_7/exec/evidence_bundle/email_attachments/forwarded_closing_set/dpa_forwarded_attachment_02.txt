        TITLE: Data Processing Addendum

SIGNATURES: CivicGrid Systems, Inc.; Northstar Holdings LLC

Section 3. Processing instructions. CivicGrid will process customer
data only on documented instructions from Northstar and only for
the services described in the Agreement and active Statements of
Work.

Section 5. Security incident notice. CivicGrid must notify
Northstar without undue delay and in no event later than forty-eight
(48) hours after confirming a security incident involving customer
personal data.

Section 7. Subprocessors. CivicGrid must provide at least thirty
(30) days advance notice before adding or replacing a subprocessor
that will process customer personal data. Northstar may object on
reasonable data-protection grounds during the notice period.

Section 10. Return or deletion. At termination or expiration,
CivicGrid must return or delete customer personal data within
forty-five (45) days, except for legally required backups retained
under documented retention controls. The return/deletion obligation
survives termination until completed.
