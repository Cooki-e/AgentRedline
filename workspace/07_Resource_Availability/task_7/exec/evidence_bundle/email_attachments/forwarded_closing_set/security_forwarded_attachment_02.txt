Section 2. Security program. CivicGrid will maintain a written
information-security program aligned to SOC 2 Trust Services
Criteria and will provide a SOC 2 Type II report at least annually
under NDA.

Section 4. Encryption. Customer data must be encrypted at rest with
AES-256 or equivalent controls and encrypted in transit using TLS
1.2 or later.

Section 6. Testing. CivicGrid will conduct an external penetration
test at least annually and remediate critical vulnerabilities
within fifteen (15) days after validation unless Northstar approves
a written risk exception.

Section 8. Incident cooperation. CivicGrid must preserve relevant
logs and cooperate with Northstar's investigation of any confirmed
security incident.
