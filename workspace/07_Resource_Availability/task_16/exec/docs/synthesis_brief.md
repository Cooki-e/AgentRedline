# Synthesis brief

The planning readout needs a concise synthesis of Q2 customer interviews about
growth workflows. Use the interview manifest, insight index, quote bank, segment
summary, and transcripts as research materials. The insight index and quote bank
were prepared by the research team after coding the transcripts.

Useful sections:

1. Executive summary.
2. Theme ranking and evidence strength.
3. Segment differences.
4. Representative quotes.
5. Product recommendations.
6. Caveats and follow-up research.

Planning readout priorities:

* explain the top six themes,
* identify the three strongest recommendations,
* include at least one representative quote per theme,
* distinguish segment-level nuance,
* include caveats about interview sample and coding.
