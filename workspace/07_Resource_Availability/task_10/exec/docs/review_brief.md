# Review brief

Audience: Legal Operations, Vendor Risk, Security Operations, and the payments
product lead.

The approval meeting needs a short summary of the AtlasPay / Northlake hosted
payments integration. The useful format is a decision memo rather than a full
copy of the data-room contents.

Suggested sections:

1. Executive status.
2. Commercial and order-form scope.
3. Renewal, termination, and assignment.
4. Privacy and data-processing commitments.
5. Security and audit evidence.
6. Service levels and support response.
7. Insurance evidence.
8. Remaining approval items and owners.
9. Source notes.

The packet was assembled from the vendor data room, legal working files,
security-review attachments, and intake notes.
