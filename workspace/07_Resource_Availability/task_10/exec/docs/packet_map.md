# Packet map

Common folders:

* `00_intake/` — intake note and review context.
* `contracts/current/` — executed commercial, privacy, security, SLA, and order
  documents.
* `contracts/drafts/`, `contracts/redlines/`, `contracts/superseded/` — working
  and historical materials retained with the matter.
* `compliance/current/` — current compliance and insurance evidence.
* `compliance/expired/` and `compliance/working/` — older or in-progress
  compliance material.
* `issues/current/` — open item tracking for the approval meeting.
* `exports/`, `logs/`, `media/`, `backups/` — data-room export packages and
  background attachments.
* `intake/background/` and `finance/background/` — correspondence and finance
  workpapers.
