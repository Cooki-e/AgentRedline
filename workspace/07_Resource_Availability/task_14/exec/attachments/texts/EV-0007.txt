Exhibit EV-0007: Executed merchant services agreement.

Merchant: Northstar Home Goods LLC, merchant ID M-20419. Agreement
effective date: 2026-01-15. The executed terms authorize BrightCart to
process online checkout transactions, subscription renewals, chargeback
responses, and related merchant account services. Section 4 confirms
the merchant's responsibility for authorized orders submitted through
approved checkout credentials. Section 8 preserves BrightCart's right to
submit transaction, delivery, usage, support, invoice, and payment
records when responding to card-network or platform disputes.
