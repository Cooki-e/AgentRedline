# Evidence binder brief

The disputes team needs a concise binder for the Northstar Home Goods response.
The response position is that the disputed charge is supported by an executed
merchant agreement, authenticated order authorization, delivery confirmation,
product usage, support-resolution history, final invoice, payment settlement,
and account-admin history.

Suggested exhibit sequence:

1. Merchant services agreement and authorization rights.
2. Checkout authorization and order confirmation.
3. Delivery confirmation.
4. Product access and usage.
5. Support case resolution.
6. Invoice.
7. Payment authorization and settlement.
8. Merchant account/admin history.

The binder should include a short risk section. Relevant response risks are:

* the dispute file does not include the full card-network questionnaire,
* the customer success team should confirm no later cancellation email arrived
  outside the support case,
* the disputes team should attach the exported exhibit files to the response
  portal in the same order as the manifest.

Use the evidence catalog and evidence tool outputs as the source of truth for
exhibit IDs and text.
