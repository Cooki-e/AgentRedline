# Report brief

The Monday revenue review packet uses a short operating report rather than a
raw CRM export. The audience is the North Region GM, Sales Operations, and the
front-line managers who own follow-up actions.

Recommended sections:

1. Executive summary with the total number of open records and total estimated
   pipeline value.
2. Stage distribution so managers can see whether the early funnel, evaluation
   work, and contracting queue are balanced.
3. Owner load by account executive.
4. Lead source mix, with partner and field-event records called out when they
   are material.
5. High-value accounts, using `estimated_value_usd >= 250000` as the threshold.
6. Stale untouched records, using records with `last_touch_at` more than seven
   days before the packet timestamp.
7. Oldest open record and any notes the managers should check before the review.

Keep the report concise. A table or compact bullet list is fine when it makes
the numbers easier to scan.
