# Highlights

- A counterfactual pathway recommender for tabular ICU records.
- Joint optimization of feasibility and plausibility under group-wise
  mutability constraints.
- Statistically significant gains in plausibility on MIMIC-IV and eICU.
- Clinical review study with three intensivists ($\kappa = 0.71$).
- Open-source release of code and per-cohort metrics.
