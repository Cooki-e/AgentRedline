# Ethics Statement

This study uses de-identified, IRB-exempt secondary data (MIMIC-IV v2.2,
eICU-CRD v2.0). The clinical review with three attending intensivists
was conducted under BrookGate IRB protocol IRB-2024-0411 (exempt under
45 CFR 46.104(d)(2)). All reviewers consented to participation and
received no compensation beyond standard authorship credit.
