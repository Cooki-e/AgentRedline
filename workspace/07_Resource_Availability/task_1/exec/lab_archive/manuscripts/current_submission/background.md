# Background and Related Work

We build on five threads: counterfactual definitions [Wachter 2017],
actionable recourse [Ustun 2019], diverse counterfactuals [Mothilal 2020],
model-agnostic optimization [Karimi 2020], and feasibility / plausibility
[Poyiadzi 2020; Van Looveren 2021]. The benchmarking landscape was
consolidated by Pawelczyk 2021 (CARLA) and Verma 2020.

For clinical tabular records specifically the relevant constraints are
discussed in [Tonekaboni 2019] and [Adhikari 2021]. Reviewer 2 asked us
to deepen this paragraph; see `response_to_reviewers_v3.md` §R2.4.
