# Results

On MIMIC-IV in-hospital mortality (Table 2 in the paper), our method
achieves an average plausibility score of 0.81 (95% CI 0.79–0.83) vs.
0.62 (0.60–0.64) for MACE and 0.71 (0.69–0.73) for the FACE baseline,
with no degradation in validity (0.94 vs. 0.95 vs. 0.93).

On eICU 30-day readmission (Table 4) the gap narrows but the ranking is
preserved (0.74 vs. 0.59 vs. 0.66).

The clinical review study (Supp. E) reports inter-rater agreement
$\kappa = 0.71$ across three intensivists on a 50-case sample.
