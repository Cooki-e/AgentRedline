# NSF IIS-2031234 — ProjectKestrel: Interpretable Time-Series Models for Wildlife Sensor Streams

**Agency:** National Science Foundation
**Program:** Information & Intelligent Systems (IIS) — Robust Intelligence
**PI:** Marisol Holst
**Co-PIs:** Anika Sato, Felix Ruiz-Carrera
**Period:** 2021-09-01 to 2024-08-31 (NCE through 2025-08-31)
**Award amount:** $498,000

**Domain:** ecology / sensor analytics

**Scope.** ProjectKestrel develops wavelet-based detectors for nocturnal
owl-call streams collected from a network of forest sensors maintained by
the Olympic Field Station. This is an ecology / sensor-analytics grant.
It has **no scope overlap** with the BrookGate clinical informatics line
of work and **must not be cited** as the funder of any clinical paper.

**Publications attributed to this grant** (see annual reporting):
- Holst et al. 2022 (Ecological Informatics)
- Holst et al. 2023 (Methods in Ecology and Evolution)

**Do not** attribute JBI-D-25-01567 or any clinical paper to
NSF IIS-2031234. The award scope statement explicitly excludes biomedical
applications.
