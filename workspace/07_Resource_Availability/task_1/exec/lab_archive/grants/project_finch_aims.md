# NIH R01-AG056789 — Specific Aims

**Aim 1.** Characterize 3-year cognitive trajectories in a rural cohort of
1,200 older adults with ≥2 chronic conditions.

**Aim 2.** Quantify the contribution of social-determinant variables to
cognitive decline.

**Aim 3.** Develop a clinical decision aid (paper-based) for primary-care
monitoring.

**Out of scope.** ICU populations; methodological / algorithmic
contributions; any work primarily about counterfactual explanations.
