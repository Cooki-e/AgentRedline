# NIH R01-LM013456 — ProjectHeron: Federated Clinical Decision Support across Critical-Access Hospitals

**Agency:** National Library of Medicine
**PI (prime):** Saanvi Desai (Univ. of Cape Cod)
**Co-Is (sub-award):** Marisol Holst (co-I, compute share)
**Period:** 2023-07-01 to 2028-06-30
**Award amount:** $1,860,000 (BrookGate sub-award: $214,000)

**Domain:** informatics / federated learning

**Scope (BrookGate sub-award).** The sub-award supports Holst's role as
a co-investigator providing compute share for the federated training
rounds; BrookGate hosts a satellite node. The sub-award does NOT cover
method development outside the Heron federation. Papers from the Heron
collaboration (e.g., Desai 2025) cite this award correctly.

**Do not** attribute the JBI submission (JBI-D-25-01567) to
R01-LM013456 — the JBI paper has no federated-learning component.
