# DOE DE-SC0023456 — Pacific Northwest Open-Compute Allowance (institutional facility grant)

**Agency:** Department of Energy
**Award holder:** Office of Research Computing, BrookGate Institute
**Period:** 2024-01-01 to 2026-12-31
**Amount:** $140,000 (institutional facility)

**Type.** This is the institute's open-compute facility allowance, NOT a
research project award. It funds shared GPU/CPU partitions used across
the institute. Per institute reporting policy, individual papers should
**acknowledge** the facility (e.g., "compute supported in part by the
BrookGate research computing facility") but **must not** cite
DE-SC0023456 as the paper's research funding source.
