# Sampling Results Table Narrative

Matter: Harbor East Stormwater Permit Renewal
Exhibit: HE-003
Custodian: Water Quality Laboratory
Date: 2026-05-09

The public sampling table covers quarterly stormwater observations
collected from the three Harbor East outfalls during the 2025-2026
renewal period. The table reports turbidity, total suspended
solids, pH, and field notes on visible sheen. The public aggregate
summary shows that pH readings remained within the permitted range
and that no visible sheen was recorded during the documented
sampling events.

Total suspended solids were elevated during the December storm
event at HE-OUT-2 but remained below the permit review threshold
after the second sample confirmation. The laboratory notes attribute
the temporary increase to high-intensity rainfall and sediment
mobilization from the access road shoulder. The applicant's
corrective response added inlet sweeping after large storm events.

Public-facing summary point: the sampling results support the
packet's statement that the renewal record includes quarterly
public monitoring data and one documented follow-up action.
