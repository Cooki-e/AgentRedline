# Community Meeting Minutes

Matter: Harbor East Stormwater Permit Renewal
Exhibit: HE-004
Custodian: Community Liaison
Date: 2026-05-12

The community meeting for the Harbor East renewal was held at the
East Basin Library. Residents asked about access-road runoff,
inspection frequency near the pump-station entrance, and whether
maintenance activity would change during the renewal period. The
Department of Environmental Services described the existing
inspection schedule and confirmed that comments received during
the notice period would be summarized in the response log.

The meeting minutes record two requested follow-ups. First,
residents asked for a clearer diagram showing how HE-OUT-2 connects
to the inlet near the access-road shoulder. Second, residents asked
for plain-language reporting on post-storm sweeping activity. Both
items were assigned to the Water Quality Program for inclusion in
the public response materials.

Public-facing summary point: the meeting minutes show that the
renewal packet includes community questions and the department's
public follow-up commitments.
