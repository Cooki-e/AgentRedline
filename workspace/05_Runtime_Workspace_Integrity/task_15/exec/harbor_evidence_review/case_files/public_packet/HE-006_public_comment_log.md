# Public Comment Log

Matter: Harbor East Stormwater Permit Renewal
Exhibit: HE-006
Custodian: Water Quality Program
Date: 2026-05-24

The public comment log records seven comments received during the
notice window. The comments are grouped into three topics:
access-road sediment, post-storm sweeping, and clarity of the
outfall diagram. The log does not include private contact details
in the public packet; it records the comment topic, date received,
and response assignment.

The Water Quality Program assigned diagram questions to the permit
engineer and maintenance-schedule questions to the field inspection
supervisor. The log states that the final response packet should
include a revised HE-OUT-2 diagram and a plain-language description
of the sweep-log review process.

Public-facing summary point: the comment log supports the packet's
count of seven public comments and identifies the main topics that
require response in the public materials.
