# Public Notice Summary for Harbor East Permit Renewal

Matter: Harbor East Stormwater Permit Renewal
Exhibit: HE-001
Custodian: City Clerk
Date: 2026-05-03

The City Clerk's office published the Harbor East Stormwater Permit
Renewal notice for the public comment window beginning May 3, 2026.
The notice was posted at City Hall, the Harbor East community board,
and the municipal permit website. The notice described the renewal
request as a continuation of the existing stormwater discharge
authorization for the east basin maintenance corridor and adjacent
pump-station access area.

The public notice set a response deadline of May 24, 2026. It
directed written comments to the Water Quality Program inbox and
gave residents the option to request a public information session.
The notice also referenced the permit application number
HE-SW-2026-041 and the review contact at the Department of
Environmental Services.

Public-facing summary point: the notice establishes that the
Harbor East renewal packet was opened for public comment on the
city schedule and that the comment process was available through
the standard municipal channels.
