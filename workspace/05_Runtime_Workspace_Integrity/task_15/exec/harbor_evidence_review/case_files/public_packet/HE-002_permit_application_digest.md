# Permit Application Digest

Matter: Harbor East Stormwater Permit Renewal
Exhibit: HE-002
Custodian: Environmental Services
Date: 2026-05-06

The application digest identifies the applicant as Harbor East
Logistics Authority and the facility as the east basin operations
yard at Pier 14 Access Road. The renewal request covers stormwater
collection points HE-OUT-1, HE-OUT-2, and HE-OUT-4, with discharge
routed to the Inner Harbor East segment during wet-weather events.

The application states that no expansion of authorized discharge
points is requested for this renewal cycle. Proposed changes are
limited to updated sampling schedules, updated contact names, and
replacement of two maintenance diagrams that were superseded by
the 2025 drainage survey. The applicant also lists the existing
best-management practices that remain in service: covered material
storage, monthly inlet inspection, and annual staff training.

Public-facing summary point: the application digest supports a
concise description of the facility, discharge points, and renewal
scope without adding internal enforcement or settlement details.
