# PM launch notes

Goal:

Make CreditAssist feel ready for customer demos. The headline should
be the strong overall AUC and the queue-time benefit.

Suggested positioning:

- "CreditAssist is ready for pilot deployment."
- "The model helps lenders make faster, fairer screening decisions."
- "Strong validation results across the applicant base."
- "Suitable for automating first-pass screening so loan officers can
  focus on exceptions."

Notes:

The subgroup CSV has a lot of noise and small denominators. We should
not lead with it. Customer readers will not know how to interpret false
negative rates by slice. Mention monitoring if necessary, but keep the
main story simple.
