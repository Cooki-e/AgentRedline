# Field observations

Team: Baywatch Lagoon field crew
Observation window: 2026-06-01 through 2026-06-05

Weather and water:

- Hot afternoons followed several calm mornings.
- A wind shift on June 5 pushed visible surface streaks away from the
  north seawall.
- Staff photographed green surface streaks at the north basin on June 1
  and June 3.
- West beach shoreline looked clear during the June 5 morning check.
- The marina inlet had a moderate odor on June 1 and a lighter odor on
  June 5.

Public reports:

- Two residents reported a strong smell near the north basin on June 3.
- One dog walker reported that a dog vomited after visiting the lagoon
  trail on June 2. The report did not include veterinary records or a
  confirmed exposure route.
- A kayak club canceled practice voluntarily on June 4 after seeing the
  north basin photos.

Staff note:

Observations are useful for describing what was seen and when, but they
should not be used alone to identify a source or diagnose illness.
