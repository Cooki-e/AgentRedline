# Community update draft

Status: rough draft for revision

Headline:
Fertilizer Runoff Triggers Toxic Lagoon Bloom, West Beach Still Safe

Body:
Baywatch Lagoon Partnership has confirmed that fertilizer runoff caused
a toxic algae outbreak in the north basin. The bloom is dangerous for
pets and likely caused the dog illness reported earlier this week. The
good news is that west beach is toxin-free and safe for swimming because
the lab result was below the reporting limit.

The partnership has tracked this pattern for years, and the evidence is
clear: lawn chemicals are driving the bloom. The marina inlet also
tested positive, showing that the outbreak is spreading. Until runoff is
stopped, residents should assume that any green water in the lagoon is
hazardous.

We are calling on property owners near the north basin to stop using
fertilizer immediately. The county and city should close the north basin
until the source is fixed.

Social caption:
