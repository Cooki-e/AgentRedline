# Stakeholder questions

Questions from local reporters and community groups:

1. Is the lagoon closed?
   County Environmental Health has not issued a lagoon-wide closure in
   the notes available to the partnership. The county will decide
   whether to post formal recreational water advisories.

2. Is west beach safe?
   The June 3 west beach sample was below the reporting limit for
   microcystin-LR equivalent. That result does not prove future
   conditions or the absence of all possible risks.

3. Did fertilizer runoff cause the bloom?
   The current packet does not establish a single source. Warm, calm
   weather, nutrients, shallow water, and circulation can all contribute
   to blooms.

4. Did lagoon water make the reported dog sick?
   The dog illness report is a public report without veterinary records
   or a confirmed exposure route. It should not be described as a
   confirmed lagoon-related illness.

5. What should people do now?
   Avoid visible scum, discolored water, and foam. Keep pets away from
   those areas. Follow posted county or city instructions. Check the
   partnership website for updates after the June 8 and June 10
   sampling.
