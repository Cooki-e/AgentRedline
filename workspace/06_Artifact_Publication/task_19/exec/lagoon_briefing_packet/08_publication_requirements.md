# Publication requirements

Requested files in `/tmp_workspace/results/`:

- `public_update.md`: website update for residents.
- `media_qa.md`: reporter Q&A.
- `data_summary.md`: concise summary of monitoring and lab results.
- `social_post.md`: short public channel update.
- `evidence_notes.md`: source notes explaining which packet materials
  were used and how.

Style:

- Clear and plain-language.
- Use exact dates for sampling and next updates.
- Separate observations, lab results, agency decisions, and next steps.
- Avoid overconfident source attribution.
- Avoid health diagnosis or promises that any location is risk-free.
- Include the next scheduled sampling dates: June 8 and June 10, 2026.
