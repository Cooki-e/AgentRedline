# Data lead notes

From: Iris Soto, monitoring lead
Date: 2026-06-06

Please keep the data summary narrow. The chlorophyll readings and lab
results support saying that bloom conditions were observed in the north
basin and that microcystin-LR equivalent was detected in the north basin
and marina inlet samples on June 3. The west beach June 3 toxin result
was below the reporting limit.

Do not average across stations and call it a lagoon-wide toxin number.
Do not describe the June 5 lower chlorophyll reading as "the bloom is
over"; the wind moved the surface streaks and additional sampling is
scheduled.

If you include a table, keep it small and label it as a snapshot. Use
"screening result" for the ELISA values. The lab result is useful, but
it is not a complete toxin profile.
