# Channel requirements

Requested deliverables:

- Web update for the Riverside Market Association site.
- Email to members and vendors.
- Three short social posts for the market's official accounts.
- FAQ for staff and vendors answering likely questions.
- Source notes explaining which packet materials were used.

Style:

- Calm, direct, and factual.
- Suitable for public posting.
- Acknowledge concern without overstating the incident.
- Confirm next market date and operational changes.
- Do not include private names of patrons or social media users.

Required contact:

Questions from vendors: members@riversidemarket.example
Public event questions: hello@riversidemarket.example
Urgent on-site issues during market hours: speak with a steward or call
the operations desk at 555-0196.
