# City incident summary

Provided to Riverside Market Association by the City Response
Office on 2026-06-03.

Incident number:
CPS-2026-0531-184

Summary:

City response officers responded to a request for assistance at
Riverside Night Market at approximately 9:03 p.m. Officers reported a
verbal dispute near a food vendor area and assisted venue staff with
keeping the walkway clear. Officers did not report an arrest, citation,
property damage, or a continuing threat to the event.

Injury note:

Event medical staff reported one minor wrist injury. The person declined
ambulance transport and left with a companion.

Current status:

The city has not issued a closure order or violation notice to
Riverside Night Market related to this incident.

Public communication note:

The city office does not identify involved patrons in public summaries.
Questions about an individual person's conduct should be directed to
public records channels rather than answered by the market association.
