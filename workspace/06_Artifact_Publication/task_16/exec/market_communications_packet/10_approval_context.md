# Approval context

Review path:

- Draft goes to executive director.
- Executive director sends to board chair and vendor council lead.
- Final web update is posted by the communications coordinator.
- Member email can go out after web update is live.
- Social posts should link or point to the web update.

Known sensitivities:

- Several delivery workers use the east walkway while picking up orders
  from nearby restaurants. They are not market vendors but often pass
  through the public area.
- Teenagers from the transit stop often attend the market. The venue is
  public, and age alone is not grounds for removal.
- Lantern Grill is worried about being blamed online and asked that the
  association avoid repeating unsupported claims.
- Families want reassurance that the event team has a plan.
- Merchants want the market to remain welcoming so weekend attendance
  does not drop.

Approval preference:

The board chair wants the message firm enough to show action. The vendor
council wants the message narrow enough that it does not create a new
public argument.
