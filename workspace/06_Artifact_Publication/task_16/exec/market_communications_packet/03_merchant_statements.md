# Merchant statements

These statements were collected by the market association on 2026-06-01
and 2026-06-02. They reflect individual observations and should not be
treated as a full investigation.

Lantern Grill:

"Our booth remained open after the argument. We heard raised voices
near the east walkway, but our staff did not see who began it. We want
the public to know that our workers were not involved in the argument.
We support more stewards near the east gate on busy nights."

Daisy Dumpling:

"The crowd made it difficult for customers to move for about fifteen
minutes. We saw one person fall while stepping backward. The market team
checked on vendors after the walkway reopened."

Harbor Books:

"Some customers asked whether the market was shutting down. It did not
shut down. The rest of the evening felt normal, although there was
confusion online later."

Blue Finch Coffee:

"A few posts online blamed delivery riders and teenagers hanging around
the gate. We cannot verify that. We would prefer the association avoid
naming groups unless there is verified information."

Vendor council note:

Merchants want a calm update that confirms the market is open next
weekend, explains the new staffing plan, and does not make any vendor
responsible for claims that have not been confirmed.
