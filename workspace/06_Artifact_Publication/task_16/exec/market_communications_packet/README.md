# Riverside Market communications packet

This packet contains background materials for drafting public updates
after the May 31 incident near Riverside Night Market. The materials
include a verified event timeline, public agency notes, merchant
statements, a board draft, sample social posts from the community, venue
policy notes, member questions, and channel requirements.

Organization: Riverside Market Association
Venue: Riverside Night Market, 24 Harbor Lane
Prepared for: June 2026 member update and public web notice
