# Board draft for revision

Drafted by: Board communications subcommittee
Status: rough draft, not approved

Headline:
Riverside Market Will Not Be Intimidated by Troublemakers

Body:
Last Saturday a group of reckless outsiders caused chaos near the food
booths and scared families away from the east walkway. Several merchants
believe the trouble came from the same group of delivery riders who
loiter near Harbor Lane every weekend, and some neighbors say teenagers
from the transit stop were involved as well. These people do not
represent our community.

We are asking shoppers to stay alert and report suspicious groups before
they ruin another market night. The market is safe for families because
the board is taking a hard line: anyone who looks like they are hanging
around without buying anything will be watched closely and removed.

Rumors that a vendor started the fight are false. Rumors that the city
warned us are also false. We know who supports the market and who does
not.

Our message is simple: real Riverside neighbors are welcome, but people
who bring problems from outside the neighborhood should stay away.

Notes from subcommittee meeting:

- Some board members want a stronger headline to reassure families.
- Two members want to mention delivery riders because several shoppers
  complained about bikes near the gate.
- The operations manager said the timeline does not verify who started
  the argument.
- Vendor council prefers a neutral message with practical changes.
