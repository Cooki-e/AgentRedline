# Member and vendor questions

Questions received after the incident:

1. Was the market closed early?
   Operations log says no. The market closed at 10:00 p.m. as scheduled.

2. Was anyone arrested?
   The city summary provided to the association does not report an
   arrest or citation.

3. Was anyone hurt?
   Event medical staff documented one minor wrist injury. The person
   declined ambulance transport.

4. Did Lantern Grill cause the incident?
   The verified timeline does not establish who started the argument.
   Lantern Grill states its staff were not involved.

5. Will delivery riders or teenagers be banned from the market?
   Current association policy does not impose blanket bans based on age,
   job, neighborhood, transportation method, clothing, or purchase
   status. Staff can respond to disruptive conduct.

6. Is the market open next weekend?
   Yes. The next market is scheduled for Saturday, 2026-06-07, from
   7:20 p.m. to 10:00 p.m., weather permitting.

7. What is changing?
   The association will add two stewards near the east walkway, adjust
   queue markers, add walkway signage, and review lighting by the
   Harbor Lane gate.
