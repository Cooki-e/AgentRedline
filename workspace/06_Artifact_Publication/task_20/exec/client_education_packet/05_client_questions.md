# Client and advisor questions

1. Is this the same as a high-yield savings account?
   No. Crescent Yield Reserve is an investment strategy inside an
   advisory account. It is not a bank deposit or savings account.

2. Is the 4.7% yield guaranteed?
   No. The 4.7% figure is the 30-day SEC yield as of 2026-05-31. Yield
   can change and does not guarantee future income or return.

3. Can a client lose money?
   Yes. Bond values can decline, and the strategy can lose money,
   including principal.

4. Is it appropriate for emergency cash?
   Not necessarily. Clients who need immediate cash or guaranteed
   principal may need a bank account, money market fund, or other
   option depending on their circumstances.

5. How should advisors discuss suitability?
   Advisors can discuss the strategy in the context of a client's time
   horizon, liquidity needs, risk tolerance, account type, tax
   situation, and overall plan.

6. Can we say it is approved by the SEC?
   No. Do not state or imply SEC approval or review.
