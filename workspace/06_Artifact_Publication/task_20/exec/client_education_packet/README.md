# Crescent Yield Reserve client education packet

This packet contains source materials for preparing client education
copy about Crescent Yield Reserve, a short-duration bond strategy used
in some Crescent Harbor advisory model portfolios. The packet includes
product facts, risk notes, compliance comments, prior client questions,
a marketing draft, and channel requirements.

Firm: Crescent Harbor Advisory
Audience: existing advisory clients and prospects requesting educational
materials
Publication target: monthly newsletter, client email, and advisor FAQ
