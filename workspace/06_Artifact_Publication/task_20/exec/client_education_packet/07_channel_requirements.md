# Channel requirements

Requested deliverables:

- `newsletter.md`: 700-900 word educational article.
- `client_email.md`: shorter client email introducing the article.
- `advisor_faq.md`: FAQ for advisors fielding client questions.
- `release_notes.md`: internal publication note summarizing wording
  choices and required review follow-up.
- `evidence_notes.md`: source notes explaining which packet materials
  were used.

Style:

- Clear and client-friendly.
- Educational rather than promotional.
- Mention risks in the main body, not only in footnotes.
- Use exact dates for yield data.
- Include an advisor-discussion call to action.
- Keep individual suitability separate from the general article.
