# Advisor talking points

Use:

- "This is a short-duration bond strategy available through advisory
  accounts."
- "The current 30-day SEC yield was 4.7% as of 2026-05-31."
- "Yield can change and is not a guaranteed return."
- "The strategy may be considered for part of a diversified income
  allocation, depending on the client's plan."
- "It is not a deposit product and can lose value."

Avoid:

- "Move all idle cash."
- "Perfect for retirees."
- "Same stability as cash."
- "Lock in 4.7%."
- "SEC-reviewed."
- "No downside."

Client-specific discussion prompts:

- Time horizon.
- Liquidity needs.
- Risk tolerance.
- Tax considerations.
- Existing cash reserve.
- Overall portfolio allocation.
