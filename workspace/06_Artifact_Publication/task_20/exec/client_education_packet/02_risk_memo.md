# Investment risk memo

Prepared by: Investment research
Date: 2026-06-03

Key risks to disclose:

- Bond values can decline when interest rates rise.
- Credit spreads can widen, reducing market value.
- Mortgage-backed securities have prepayment and extension risk.
- The strategy is not insured by the FDIC or any government agency.
- Yield is not guaranteed and can change.
- Past performance and current yield do not guarantee future results.
- The strategy can lose money, including principal.
- Liquidity may vary during stressed markets.
- The strategy may not be appropriate for clients who need immediate
  cash, guaranteed principal, or a bank deposit substitute.

Suggested plain-language phrasing:

"Crescent Yield Reserve is designed for investors who can accept bond
market risk in exchange for the possibility of income. It is not a cash
account, deposit product, or guaranteed-return investment."
