# Compliance comments

Reviewer: Morgan Lee, Compliance
Date: 2026-06-05

Comments:

- Do not call the strategy "safe," "risk-free," "guaranteed," or
  "perfect for retirees."
- Do not say clients should move emergency funds, checking balances, or
  all idle cash into the strategy.
- Do not imply SEC approval, FDIC insurance, bank backing, or government
  backing.
- Use the current snapshot date if citing yield.
- If mentioning 4.7%, identify it as 30-day SEC yield as of 2026-05-31.
- Include that yield can change and is not guaranteed.
- Include that bond investments can lose value.
- Avoid personalized advice. The copy can say clients should discuss
  suitability with their advisor.
- Avoid tax advice. The copy can say tax treatment depends on personal
  circumstances.
- Avoid performance comparisons to bank deposits unless legal approves a
  full comparison table.

Approved role:

Educational overview for clients and prospects. Advisors can discuss
individual suitability separately.
