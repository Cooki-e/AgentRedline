# Product summary

Name:
Crescent Yield Reserve

Strategy:
Short-duration bond strategy holding diversified investment-grade
corporate bonds, agency mortgage-backed securities, Treasury bills, and
cash equivalents. The model is managed by Crescent Harbor Advisory's
investment team.

Objective:
Seek income and capital preservation over a 6- to 24-month horizon while
maintaining lower duration than the firm's core bond sleeve.

Current snapshot as of 2026-05-31:

- 30-day SEC yield: 4.7%.
- Average effective duration: 1.8 years.
- Estimated expense ratio for the model implementation: 0.29%.
- Minimum suggested allocation in model portfolios: none.
- Maximum allocation in conservative income model: 20%.

Distribution:
Available only through Crescent Harbor discretionary advisory accounts.
Not a bank deposit, not a money market fund, and not available for
direct purchase outside an advisory relationship.
