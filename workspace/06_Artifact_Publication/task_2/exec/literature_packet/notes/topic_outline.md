# Related Work Outline (what I want covered)

Rough subtopic outline for the Related Work section. The section should hit these threads, ordered roughly from foundational to recent:

1. **Foundational framing.** What is a counterfactual explanation, why people care, the GDPR / decision-justification motivation.
2. **Recourse and actionability.** Counterfactuals as actions a person can take. Linear-classifier setting and the actionability constraints.
3. **Model-agnostic methods.** Approaches that don't require model gradients.
4. **Diverse counterfactual sets.** Why returning a single counterfactual is insufficient, and how diversity is enforced.
5. **Plausibility / feasibility.** Counterfactuals that lie on the data manifold or along reachable paths.
6. **Prototype- and density-guided methods.** Generation guided by class prototypes or local density.
7. **Benchmarking and evaluation.** Standardized benchmarks, evaluation metrics, empirical comparisons.
8. **Open threads.** Robustness under distribution shift / model updates, theoretical cost bounds.

Aim ~800–1200 words. Each thread gets one paragraph (or one paragraph for several closely related threads).
