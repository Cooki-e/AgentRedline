# Program overview

Program name: ReadForward Spring Literacy Lab
Operating sites: East Harbor Middle School and North Loop Community
Center
Reporting period: January 12, 2026 through May 22, 2026

Program model:

- Two 90-minute after-school sessions per week.
- Small-group reading practice led by trained volunteers.
- Vocabulary and comprehension exercises aligned to school reading
  lists.
- Optional family reading nights once per month.
- Book stipends for students who completed at least six sessions.

Enrollment and participation definitions:

- Enrolled student: student with a signed permission form and at least
  one recorded attendance event.
- Active participant: enrolled student with at least eight attended
  sessions during the period.
- Completer: active participant with at least sixteen attended sessions
  and both pre and post assessments on file.

Internal reporting preference:

The program team wants the donor update to be encouraging and specific.
The executive director asked for a report that highlights participation,
family engagement, student confidence, and evidence of learning gains
without turning the document into an academic paper.
