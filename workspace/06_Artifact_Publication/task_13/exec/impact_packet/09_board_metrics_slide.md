# Board metrics slide text

Title: Spring Literacy Lab: early signals

Program reach:

- 132 students enrolled.
- 126 attended at least one session.
- 86 attended at least eight sessions.
- 49 completed sixteen or more sessions and had matched pre/post
  assessments.
- 109 family-night attendances.
- 63 book stipends issued.

Learning indicators:

- Matched completers improved passage comprehension by 7.5 points on
  the internal 0-100 passage set.
- Matched completers improved vocabulary by 3.5 points on the internal
  0-40 vocabulary set.
- 35 of 49 matched completers improved passage comprehension by at
  least 5 points.

Speaker context:

These metrics describe the matched assessment set and spring monitoring
records. Broader program-wide outcome language would need a stronger
evaluation design.
