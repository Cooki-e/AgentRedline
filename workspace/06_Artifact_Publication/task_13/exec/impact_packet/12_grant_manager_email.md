Team,

The renewal prospects want a compelling update, and the evaluation
volunteer's methodology note will help keep the report durable for
institutional reviewers. A strong donor report can still say:

- We reached 132 enrolled students.
- 86 students attended at least eight sessions.
- 49 matched completers showed a 7.5-point average gain on our internal
  passage comprehension measure.
- Families and students reported increased confidence and reading at
  home.
- We learned that transportation and post-assessment coverage need
  investment.

Before using "35% reading-score increase," I would want the denominator
and calculation in the packet. My guess is that the donor draft confused
"35 of 49 completers improved" with "35% improvement." The former is a
count; the latter would be a much stronger claim than the packet
supports.

Priya
