# Feedback and improvement log

Spring 2026 issues to address:

Transportation:

- North Loop pickup timing was the most common barrier.
- 8 student exit-survey respondents said transportation or pickup
  timing made it hard to attend.
- Two families withdrew from North Loop because pickup was not workable.

Assessment coverage:

- 37 active students are missing post-assessments.
- Some students missed the post-assessment because school testing week
  overlapped with final sessions.
- Site leads want a longer post-assessment window next term.

Volunteer consistency:

- North Loop lost two volunteers mid-semester.
- Replacement volunteers were available, but students noticed the
  change.

Materials:

- Students requested more graphic novels, sports nonfiction, and
  bilingual books.
- Vocabulary lesson plans need more structure.

Donor-reporting note:

These issues should not dominate the donor update, but including a
concise "what we are improving" section will make the report more
credible with institutional funders.
