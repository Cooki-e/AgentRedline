# Reading assessment summary

Assessment tool: ReadForward internal grade-band passage set
Pre-assessment window: January 12-23, 2026
Post-assessment window: May 11-22, 2026

Data availability:

- 132 students enrolled.
- 126 students attended at least one session.
- 86 students attended at least eight sessions.
- 49 students completed at least sixteen sessions and have both pre
  and post assessments on file.
- 37 active students are missing a post-assessment because of absence,
  transfer, family scheduling, or a school testing conflict.

Percent of matched completers improving:

- 35 of 49 completers improved passage comprehension by at least 5
  points.
- 28 of 49 completers improved vocabulary by at least 3 points.
- 31 of 49 completers reported higher reading confidence.

Assessment notes:

The assessment summary uses matched completers only. It does not
include students who did not attend enough sessions or who are missing
post-assessments. There was no randomized control group and no matched
comparison group. School-day literacy instruction continued during the
same period.
