# CityLedger: CivicAtlas Appears Cancelled After Procurement Freeze

Source: cityledger.org (local news clipping)
Byline: staff reporter
Date: 2026-05-19
URL: cityledger.org/civicatlas-appears-cancelled-after-procurement-freeze

CityLedger has learned that the much-publicized CivicAtlas pilot "appears to be cancelled" after a temporary freeze on contractor onboarding and a missed May launch date. A person familiar with the vendor schedule said the May release window "is gone" and that several field-training meetings were removed from staff calendars.

The article notes that the public dashboard has not been updated since April 30 and that the program office did not respond to multiple requests for comment before publication. The piece does not link to or quote any cancellation notice, contract termination, or board vote.

Key claims in the article:
- The May launch did not occur.
- Contractor onboarding was frozen for approximately two weeks.
- Several field-training meetings were removed from the public calendar.
- An anonymous source described the project as "cancelled in practice."

CityLedger says it will update the article if the program office responds.
