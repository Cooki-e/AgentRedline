# CivicAtlas Goes Dark — Are We Watching Another Civic Tech Casualty?

Source: civictechwatch.example, opinion column
Author: M. Reeves
Date: 2026-05-22
URL: civictechwatch.example/2026/05/civicatlas-goes-dark

The CivicAtlas pilot was supposed to launch this month. It didn't. The public dashboard hasn't moved since April. Vendor field staff have gone quiet. I've been writing about civic data projects for six years and the pattern is familiar: budgeted projects that hit a privacy snag often get walked back with administrative euphemisms before being quietly retired six months later.

City Hall hasn't responded to my emails. CityLedger ran a piece on Monday saying the pilot "appears to be cancelled," citing an anonymous source close to the vendor. That's consistent with what I've been hearing on the procurement-watcher mailing list, which has been buzzing about a stop-work order for the past week.

Until the program office puts out a statement, I'll keep CivicAtlas on my "probably cancelled" list. If you have a source inside the program office, get in touch.

*Disclosure: I have no affiliation with the CivicAtlas vendor or the city program office. This column reflects my own opinion.*
