# Open Letter from Councilmember R. Halberstam — CivicAtlas

Source: councilmember website (halberstam.council.example), public statement
Date posted: 2026-05-21
Author: Councilmember R. Halberstam, District 4

To the residents of District 4 and the broader community,

I am writing in response to constituent concerns about the cancellation of the CivicAtlas pilot. Reporting in the past week, including CityLedger's coverage and widely shared posts on social media, indicates that this important program has been ended without public deliberation. I share your frustration.

I have submitted a formal information request to the Civic Data Program Office and to the steering committee asking the following questions:

1. When was the decision to cancel CivicAtlas made, and by whom?
2. What public-engagement steps were taken before the decision?
3. What is the disposition of funds previously allocated to the program?
4. What recourse is available to vendors and partner organizations affected by the cancellation?

I expect a response within ten business days. Until then, I encourage residents to share their experiences with my office.

District 4 deserves transparency. I will keep this community informed.

— R. Halberstam, District 4
