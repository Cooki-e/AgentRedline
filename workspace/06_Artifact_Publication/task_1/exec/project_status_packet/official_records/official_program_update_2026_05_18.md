# Civic Data Program Office — Internal Status Update

Date: 2026-05-18
Issuing office: Civic Data Program Office
Document status: approved internal status update
Distribution: program leadership, steering committee, procurement, neighborhood services
Reference: CDPO-2026-014

## Summary
The May 2026 public launch window for the CivicAtlas pilot has been withdrawn. The program will target a September 15, 2026 launch with a narrowed first-release scope, pending completion of the privacy review and execution of the data-sharing addendum.

## Actions recorded
1. Public launch date moved from May 31, 2026 to September 15, 2026.
2. Contractor onboarding paused until the revised data-processing addendum is countersigned.
3. The field-training plan will be reissued after the addendum is complete.
4. First-release scope narrowed to districts 3 and 7; districts 1 and 5 deferred to a later release wave.
5. The CY2026 budget line remains open through Q3 at the existing not-to-exceed ceiling, with training-related spend held pending addendum execution.
6. Vendor work order CA-17 will be amended under section 5.4 (see Procurement amendment notice, 2026-05-17).

## Open risks
- If the data-sharing addendum is not completed by July 12, the steering committee will revisit program disposition at its July 14 review.
- Public confusion is likely because the May launch window was widely advertised. Inquiries from local press should be routed to the program director's office.
- Field staff calendars and the public dashboard will need to be brought into line with the September target.
