## Agenda item 2: Program disposition
The committee considered three motions:

- **M1:** Authorize the September 15, 2026 launch with the narrowed first-release scope (districts 3 and 7).
- **M2:** Direct the program office to draft a scope-reduction plan limiting field activity to data quality remediation only.

- M3: placed on the agenda by the Chair. Did not receive a second; tabled without prejudice.
