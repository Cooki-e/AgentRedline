## Purpose
This memo lays out three de-scope scenarios for the CivicAtlas pilot in response to the slipped May launch window. The scenarios are illustrative for the steering committee's decision. None has been adopted.

## Scenarios
**Scenario A — Hold and resume:**
- Hold May launch; resume on revised September window with narrowed first-release scope (districts 3 and 7).
- Q3 budget line: unchanged ceiling; training spend held until addendum execution.
- Estimated underspend vs original plan: ~12%.

**Scenario B — Reduce to data quality only:**
- Continue data quality remediation; suspend field training and public-facing release in CY2026.
- Q3 budget line: reduce training/field line by ~40%; redirect to next cycle.
- Estimated underspend vs original plan: ~28%.

**Scenario C — Wind-down:**
- Close out vendor work order under section 7 (termination for convenience).
- Q3 budget line: hold residual to cover close-out and 90 days of data retention.
- Estimated underspend vs original plan: ~62%.

## Recommendation
Budget office takes no position. The choice is a programmatic and policy call for the steering committee.
