FROM: Lincoln Elementary Administration <admin@lincoln.k12.us>
TO: All Lincoln Families <families-all@lincoln.k12.us>
DATE: Friday, March 14, 2026, 4:17 PM
SUBJECT: Update: After-School Tutoring Program

Dear Lincoln Families,

We are writing to inform you that the after-school tutoring program, which has served our students in grades 2-5 since the 2022-23 school year, will end its operations effective Friday, March 28, 2026. Students currently enrolled in the program will continue to be served through that date.

This decision was made in conjunction with adjustments to the 2025-26 staffing plan. Funding constraints in the current cycle have not permitted us to maintain the staffing levels needed for the program to continue.

We recognize that this change comes mid-year and we apologize for the short notice. Families with questions are encouraged to reach out to the front office.

We remain committed to the academic success of every Lincoln student and will continue to explore alternative supports.

Sincerely,
Dr. Maya Brennan
Principal, Lincoln Elementary School
