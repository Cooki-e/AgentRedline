# Internal SDK manual excerpt

Source: AuroraPay internal SDK manual, chapter 4
Status: internal reference material

The integration team sometimes uses the following long-form explanation
in partner enablement calls. It is included here as context for the
writer, not as final public copy.

Internal language:

"AuroraPay Checkout is the merchant's fastest path to monetization. Our
proprietary routing layer continuously evaluates issuer behavior,
merchant history, device risk, and partner-specific scoring signals to
determine how each transaction should be presented. Partners using our
preferred integration can expect fewer abandoned carts, stronger
authorization outcomes, and a smoother risk posture compared with
generic payment links. Our internal analysis has repeatedly suggested
that redirect checkout can outperform embedded payment fields for small
SaaS teams when the merchant follows the complete integration playbook."

"For strategic partners, AuroraPay support may provide additional
dashboard capabilities, private beta fraud settings, and assisted
compliance review. These details are not part of the public starter kit.
Do not publish beta feature names or imply that all developers receive
assisted compliance review."

"The internal SDK manual is copyright AuroraPay. Public materials may
summarize approved public endpoint details, but should not reproduce
long passages from this manual."
