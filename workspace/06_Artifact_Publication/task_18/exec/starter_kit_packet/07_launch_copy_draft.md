Headline:
Certified AuroraPay Checkout Starter Kit for Faster SaaS Revenue

Body:
This official kit gives small SaaS teams a compliance-ready path to
payment acceptance. Built on Express and Node.js, the certified example
includes everything needed to improve authorization outcomes and launch
with confidence. AuroraPay's proprietary routing and partner-grade risk
posture help merchants reduce abandoned carts compared with generic
payment links.

Download the starter kit and copy the sample code into your app. The
integration uses the proven checkout pattern from our internal SDK
manual, plus a helper adapted from the PayDemo community project.

starter kit.
