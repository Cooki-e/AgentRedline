# Standard footer language

Use a concise footer on public and customer-facing materials:

"NimbusDesk recommends that administrators apply security updates
promptly and review deployment-specific logs when evaluating account
activity. This advisory is based on information available to NimbusDesk
as of the publication date and may be updated as additional information
becomes available."

Additional wording approved for the FAQ:

"For self-hosted deployments, log availability and retention vary by
customer environment. NimbusDesk support can help interpret relevant
application events, but administrators should also review proxy and
object-store records under their own retention policies."

Avoid absolute statements about every customer environment. The public
materials can describe the hosted log review and the self-hosted review
steps, but the footer should leave room for additional information.
