# Patient advocate comments

Reviewer: Jordan Miles, Northbridge Patient Advisory Council
Date: 2026-06-04

Comments on recruitment copy:

- People with eczema are tired of being promised relief. Please write in
  plain language and avoid miracle language.
- Mention placebo plainly. Patients should not discover later that they
  might not get the active study product.
- Include the visit burden. Six clinic visits plus follow-up matters for
  working patients and caregivers.
- Say that the product is investigational in the flyer, not only in the
  consent form.
- Compensation is helpful, but it should not sound like a job or a
  guaranteed payment for signing up.
- "May or may not help" is clearer than "promising."
- Please include a sentence that people can ask questions without
  committing to join.
