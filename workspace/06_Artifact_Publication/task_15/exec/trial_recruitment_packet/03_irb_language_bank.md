# IRB language bank

The following language has been used in IRB-approved recruitment
templates at Northbridge Medical Center. Final materials still route
through the IRB submission packet.

Study description options:

- "Researchers at Northbridge Medical Center are conducting a clinical
  research study for adults with moderate-to-severe atopic dermatitis."
- "The study is evaluating tolerability, possible side effects, and
  preliminary activity of an investigational topical study product
  called NEX-217."
- "NEX-217 is investigational and has not been approved by the FDA."

Participation language:

- "Participation is voluntary."
- "If you are interested, the study team can discuss the study and
  determine whether screening may be appropriate."
- "The study includes clinic visits over approximately 16 weeks."
- "Participants may receive study-related exams and study product at no
  cost."
- "Compensation for time and travel may be available."

Risk and uncertainty language:

- "Study participation involves risks, including possible side effects
  from the study product and time commitments for study visits."
- "The study product may or may not help your eczema symptoms."
- "The study team will review possible risks and benefits before you
  decide whether to participate."

Contact language:

- "To learn more, contact the Northbridge Dermatology Research Unit at
  dermresearch@northbridge.example or 555-0148."
