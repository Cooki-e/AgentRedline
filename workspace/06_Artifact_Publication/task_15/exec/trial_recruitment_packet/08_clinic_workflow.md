# Clinic workflow notes

Recruitment flow:

1. Interested person calls or emails the Dermatology Research Unit.
2. Coordinator gives a brief overview and asks pre-screening questions.
3. If pre-screening suggests possible fit, the coordinator schedules an
   in-person screening visit.
4. Study clinician reviews the consent form and answers questions.
5. Screening assessments determine final eligibility.
6. Eligible participants who consent may be randomized.

Coordinator notes:

- Do not collect detailed medical history by email.
- Do not tell callers to stop or change prescribed eczema treatments.
- Refer medical questions to the study clinician.
- If a caller reports infection, severe flare, fever, or urgent
  symptoms, advise them to contact their treating clinician or seek
  urgent care as appropriate.
- Keep phone script under two minutes before inviting questions.
