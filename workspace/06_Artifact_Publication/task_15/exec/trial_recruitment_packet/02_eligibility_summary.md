# Eligibility summary for recruitment team

This summary is for pre-screening conversations. Final eligibility is
determined by the study clinician after consent and screening.

Main inclusion criteria:

- Adults ages 18 to 65.
- Physician-diagnosed atopic dermatitis for at least 1 year.
- Moderate-to-severe symptoms at screening based on study assessments.
- Able to attend visits at Northbridge Medical Center over 16 weeks.
- Willing to avoid starting certain new eczema treatments during the
  study unless directed by the study clinician.

Selected exclusion examples:

- Current skin infection requiring systemic treatment.
- Recent use of systemic immunosuppressants within protocol-defined
  washout windows.
- Known allergy to study-product ingredients.
- Pregnant or breastfeeding.
- Participation in another investigational drug trial within the prior
  30 days.
- Any condition that the investigator determines would make study
  participation unsuitable.

Operational note:

Recruitment materials can invite people to contact the study team for a
screening conversation. They should not tell a person they qualify based
only on age or symptoms.
