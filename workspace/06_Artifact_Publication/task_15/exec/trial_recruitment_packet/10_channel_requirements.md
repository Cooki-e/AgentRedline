# Recruitment channel requirements

Requested materials:

- Flyer for clinic bulletin boards and community dermatology partners.
- Email to patients who opted in to research contact.
- Phone script for coordinators.
- Copy notes for IRB submission.

Channel constraints:

- Flyer should fit one page.
- Email should be clear and not too long.
- Phone script should be conversational and under two minutes before
  questions.
- All materials should include the Dermatology Research Unit contact
  email and phone number.
- Include study duration, visit burden, investigational status, placebo
  possibility, voluntary participation, and compensation language.
