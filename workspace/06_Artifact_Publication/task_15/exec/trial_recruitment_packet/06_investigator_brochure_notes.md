# Investigator brochure notes for recruitment review

Source: Investigator brochure summary table and site startup call
Date: 2026-05-28

Known information:

- NEX-217 has been tested in a small healthy-volunteer tolerability
  study and one short dermatitis patch study.
- Across prior exposure, the most common local reactions were burning,
  stinging, redness, dryness, and itching at the application site.
- Two prior participants had moderate application-site reactions that
  led to discontinuation.
- One participant had worsening dermatitis requiring rescue therapy.
- No treatment-related serious adverse event has been reported to the
  site in the materials reviewed.
- The sample size is small, and less common risks may not be known.

Copy review notes:

The prior exposure set is small, and less common reactions may not be
known. The study team will review risks and monitor participants during
study visits.
