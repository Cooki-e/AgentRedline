# Protocol synopsis

Study title:
A Phase 1b/2a randomized, double-blind, placebo-controlled study of
NEX-217 in adults with moderate-to-severe atopic dermatitis.

Sponsor:
Novexia Therapeutics

Site:
Northbridge Medical Center Dermatology Research Unit

Investigational product:
NEX-217, an investigational topical immunomodulator.

Study design:

- Approximately 96 participants across 8 sites.
- Northbridge target: 12 to 16 enrolled participants.
- Participants randomized 2:1 to NEX-217 or vehicle placebo.
- Treatment period: 12 weeks.
- Follow-up period: 4 weeks after treatment.
- Study visits: screening, baseline/randomization, weeks 2, 4, 8, 12,
  and follow-up at week 16.
- Participants continue standard skin-care routines allowed by the
  protocol, including non-medicated emollients.

Primary objectives:

- Evaluate tolerability and adverse events associated with NEX-217.
- Estimate preliminary changes in eczema severity scores to inform
  later-phase study design.

Status:

NEX-217 has not been approved by the Food and Drug Administration for
atopic dermatitis or any other indication. The study is designed to
gather tolerability, adverse-event, and preliminary activity data.
