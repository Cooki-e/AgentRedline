# Participant reimbursement policy

Study: NEX-217
Site: Northbridge Medical Center

Reimbursement:

- Up to $75 per completed study visit for time and travel.
- Reimbursement is not paid for simply contacting the study team.
- Reimbursement is not a guarantee of eligibility or enrollment.
- Missed visits are not reimbursed.
- Parking vouchers may be available for in-person clinic visits.

Recruitment language:

"Compensation for time and travel may be available for completed study
visits" is the preferred short wording.
