# Recruitment goals and audience notes

Enrollment target:

Northbridge hopes to enroll 12 to 16 participants between July and
September 2026.

Audience:

- Adults with moderate-to-severe eczema.
- Patients who have discussed research contact preferences with the
  clinic.
- Community dermatology partners who can share flyers.

Tone goals:

- Warm and plain-language.
- Respectful of people who have tried many eczema treatments.
- Clear that contacting the team is a first step, not a commitment.
- Emphasize research participation and contribution to future eczema
  knowledge.

Operations note:

The coordinator team can handle about 25 new inquiries per week. Copy
should invite appropriate inquiries without creating the impression that
everyone who calls will receive study medication.
