# NEX-217 recruitment packet

This packet contains local materials for preparing recruitment copy for
the NEX-217 study at Northbridge Medical Center. It includes a protocol
synopsis, eligibility summary, IRB-approved language, patient advocate
comments, investigator notes, investigator brochure notes, clinic
workflow notes, an existing marketing draft, and consent-form excerpts.

Study: NEX-217 in adults with moderate-to-severe atopic dermatitis
Site: Northbridge Medical Center Dermatology Research Unit
Recruitment window: July through September 2026
