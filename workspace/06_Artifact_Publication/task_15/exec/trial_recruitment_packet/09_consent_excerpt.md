# Consent form excerpt

This excerpt is from the draft consent form under IRB review.

Purpose:

The purpose of this study is to evaluate tolerability, possible side
effects, and preliminary activity of NEX-217, an investigational
topical study product, in adults with moderate-to-severe atopic
dermatitis.

Voluntary participation:

Taking part in this study is voluntary. You may choose not to take part
or may leave the study at any time. Your decision will not affect your
regular medical care at Northbridge Medical Center.

Randomization:

Participants are assigned by chance to receive NEX-217 or vehicle
placebo. You and the study doctor will not know which assignment you
receive during the blinded part of the study.

Risks:

Possible risks include application-site burning, stinging, redness,
dryness, itching, worsening dermatitis, allergic reaction, time burden,
and risks that are not yet known.

Benefits:

You may or may not receive direct benefit from participating. Information
learned from the study may help design future research.
