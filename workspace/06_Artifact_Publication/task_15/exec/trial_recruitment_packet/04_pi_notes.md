# Principal investigator notes

PI: Dr. Elena Varga
Date: 2026-06-02

The clinic needs recruitment materials that are easy for patients to
understand. We need enough interest to fill the site target, but we do
not want a flood of people who think this is standard eczema treatment.

Talking points:

- Adults with moderate-to-severe atopic dermatitis may contact the
  research unit.
- The product is investigational.
- Tolerability and adverse-event monitoring are primary objectives.
- Some participants receive placebo vehicle.
- The study team will determine eligibility.
- Continue usual care unless the study clinician gives instructions.

Practical details:

- Study visits occur at Northbridge Medical Center.
- The full study is about 16 weeks including follow-up.
- Reimbursement is available up to $75 per completed visit for time and
  travel.
- Recruitment should be inclusive and not imply cost-free treatment is
  the main reason to join.

Phrases I like:

- "Help advance eczema research."
- "Learn whether screening may be appropriate."
- "Study-related care at no cost."
- "Voluntary research study."
