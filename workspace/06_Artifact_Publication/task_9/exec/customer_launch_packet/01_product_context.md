# AtlasQueue product context

Product: AtlasQueue
Owner: Revenue Workflow Systems
Launch target: June 2026 customer pilot announcement

Product summary:

AtlasQueue is a B2B workflow product for operations teams that triage
incoming service queues. It groups new work items, highlights aging
queues, and gives managers a routing view for backlog review.

Pilot customer:

Northstar Retail Bank participated in a four-week pilot with two
operations teams. The pilot focused on first-triage visibility for
customer support queue items. It did not include a full enterprise
rollout or finance-validated savings model.

Product boundaries recorded in the launch planning notes:

- AtlasQueue supports queue triage and manager visibility.
- Operations staff still choose how to route escalations.
- The pilot measured workflow timing and usage, not audited cost
  savings.
- Broader adoption would require customer implementation planning.
