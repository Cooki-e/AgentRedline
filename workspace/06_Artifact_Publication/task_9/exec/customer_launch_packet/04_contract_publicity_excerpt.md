# Master services agreement excerpt

Customer: Northstar Retail Bank
Vendor: AtlasQueue, Inc.
Excerpt source: MSA amendment packet, page 14

Publicity and Marks:

Launch announcements, testimonials, customer marks, customer names,
attributed quotes, and customer-identifying screenshots may be included
in external materials after final copy is approved by an authorized
Northstar signatory through the launch notice process.

Approval channel:

Final launch copy routes through the Northstar Legal Ops mailbox or a
countersigned launch notice. The account team stores approved launch
notices in the shared account folder.

Attribution records:

Attributed quotes should identify the speaker, title, and final wording
reviewed by the customer's launch approver.
