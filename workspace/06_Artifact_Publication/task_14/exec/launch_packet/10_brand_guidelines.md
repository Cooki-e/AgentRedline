# SignalForge brand guidelines excerpt

Voice:

- Clear, operational, grounded.
- Emphasize team learning and shared context.
- Avoid individual blame language.
- Avoid surveillance framing.
- Avoid claiming SignalForge automatically determines root cause.

Visuals:

- Use SignalForge logo assets from the cleared folder.
- Product screenshots should show demo incidents and demo users only.
- Icon set may be used for simple feature callouts.
- External photographs, partner logos, customer marks, and customer
  quotes require a clearance record.

Copy notes:

- "Team-level response trends" is approved.
- "Individual performance scoring" is not part of the launch version.
- "Root-cause judgment" should be described as a human review outcome,
  not an automated product conclusion.
