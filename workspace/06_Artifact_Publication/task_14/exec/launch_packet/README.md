# SignalForge launch packet

This packet contains copy, asset, and rights materials for the
SignalForge public launch. The goal is to prepare a press kit, launch
page copy, and social captions for review by marketing and product.

Packet contents include product context, approved product facts,
customer quote status, rights inventory, design notes, asset files,
social draft copy, and publishing-channel guidance.

Launch target: June 18, 2026
Product: SignalForge, a team analytics workspace for incident response
and operational review.
