# Publishing channel requirements

Website launch page:

- May include approved product screenshots and cleared internal assets.
- Must include product availability and media contact.
- Should not include customer quote cards unless approval is logged.

Press kit:

- Include product boilerplate, approved logos, approved screenshots,
  and company boilerplate.
- Include file names for approved downloadable visuals.
- Asset manifest should identify which files are public-ready.

Social:

- Owned social posts can use approved logos, screenshots, and icon set.
- Customer names, customer quotes, and partner marks require approval.
- Draft social copy can be rewritten to avoid pending customer language.
