# Product FAQ

Q: What problem does SignalForge solve?

A: Teams often reconstruct incidents from ticket comments, chat
messages, deployment notes, and status-page updates. SignalForge brings
those records into one incident review workspace so teams can build a
timeline, understand handoffs, and close follow-up actions.

Q: Does SignalForge score individual responders?

A: The launch version focuses on team-level response trends and
workflow visibility. It is not designed as an individual employee
scoring product.

Q: Which integrations are available at launch?

A: GitHub Issues, Jira, PagerDuty, Slack, and StatusHub.

Q: What is in beta?

A: Enterprise SSO and audit-log export are available to beta customers.

Q: What visuals are ready?

A: The sanitized dashboard overview, timeline detail screenshot,
SignalForge logos, and internal icon set are ready for public launch.
