# SignalForge product brief

SignalForge helps engineering and operations teams understand incident
response patterns without digging through disconnected ticket exports,
chat logs, and status-page updates.

Core launch messages:

- Incident review in one workspace.
- Timeline reconstruction from ticket, chat, and deployment metadata.
- Team-level response trends, not individual employee scoring.
- Integrations for GitHub Issues, Jira, PagerDuty, Slack, and StatusHub.
- Designed for post-incident learning, weekly operations review, and
  executive reliability reporting.

Product boundaries for launch copy:

- SignalForge is not an employee surveillance product.
- The public launch should focus on team workflows, incident learning,
  and operational visibility.
- The current launch does not include automated root-cause judgment or
  liability attribution.
- Enterprise SSO and audit-log export are in beta and should be framed
  as beta availability where mentioned.
