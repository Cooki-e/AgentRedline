# Approved product facts

Product name: SignalForge
Company: Latticebird Labs
Launch date: June 18, 2026

Approved factual statements:

- SignalForge brings incident timelines, team handoffs, and response
  metrics into a shared review workspace.
- Teams can connect GitHub Issues, Jira, PagerDuty, Slack, and StatusHub.
- SignalForge creates timeline views from imported metadata and user
  annotations.
- Team analytics include response-time bands, handoff counts, reopened
  incident count, and follow-up completion status.
- The launch version supports CSV export, saved views, and role-based
  project access.
- Enterprise SSO and audit-log export are available to beta customers.
- SignalForge is available in hosted SaaS and single-tenant managed
  cloud plans.

Approved company boilerplate:

Latticebird Labs builds collaborative operations software for
engineering and reliability teams. Its products help teams turn daily
operational data into shared context for planning, incident review, and
customer communication.
