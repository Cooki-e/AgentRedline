>
> **Section 1 — The Buying Environment.** Gartner argues that the
> procurement automation buying environment has fundamentally shifted

> incumbent vendors face a structurally more difficult renewal

> meaningful consolidation. Vendors that re-architect around
> outcome-aligned pricing will fare best.
>
