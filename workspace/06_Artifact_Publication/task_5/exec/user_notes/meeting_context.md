## Why we're meeting
We have been asked by the board, as part of the FY27 planning cycle,
to come back with a position on three questions:

1. **Do we continue investing in vertical-SaaS expansion** at the
   current rate, or do we redirect a portion of that spend toward
   workflow defense in our existing book?
2. **Should we accelerate the pricing-model transition** off per-seat
   licensing, and if so on what timeline?
3. **Are we exposed on the exit-portability story** — i.e., is our
   open export API a competitive feature or a liability?

The board wants a recommendation, not a position paper, by end of
June.

## What the briefing is for
The briefing is the **starting point** for the discussion. It is not
the discussion. It needs to do three things:

- Surface what *we* think on each of the three questions, in language
  the CFO and CRO will engage with.
- Flag where we genuinely disagree internally (Aanya and Tom are
  expected to take different positions on the per-seat question).
- Identify what we'd need to learn between Monday and the end of June
  to feel confident giving the board a recommendation.

It does **not** need to be a literature review. The NextStack piece
is one input among several; the meeting will not run more efficiently
if the briefing reproduces it.
