# Bayford Housing Help Center notes

Riley called the Bayford Housing Help Center intake line on 2026-06-04.
These are Riley's notes from the call.

Intake volunteer:

- The city help center does not represent tenants, but it can explain
  common deposit dispute steps.
- A written dispute letter is usually the first step.
- Include the move-out date, the deposit amount, the returned amount,
  the deductions, and why each deduction is disputed.
- Attach or reference the move-in condition report, move-out photos,
  cleaning receipt, key receipt, and any repair emails.
- Ask for invoices and photos if they were not included.
- Keep the tone factual. The volunteer said not to accuse fraud unless
  there is evidence.
- If the landlord does not respond, options may include small-claims
  court or a tenant legal clinic.
