Title: "Demand letter that got my deposit back in Northbridge"

I used this in Northbridge and my landlord paid immediately. Edit it
however you need:

"You violated the 14-day deposit law and now owe me three times my
deposit plus attorney fees. Your deductions are illegal and fraudulent.
If you do not pay within 48 hours, I will file criminal charges, report
you to every housing agency, and make sure you are barred from renting
apartments again. You must pay the full deposit, treble damages, and
my court costs."

Comment from another user:

Check your own city because some places use 21 days, not 14. Also,
triple damages are not the rule everywhere. I still like the aggressive
tone because landlords only listen when tenants sound serious.
