# Harborview Court lease excerpts

Tenant: Riley Chen
Landlord: Carden Property Management
Unit: Harborview Court Unit 4B
Lease term: 2025-05-16 through 2026-05-15

Section 5 - Security deposit:

Tenant paid a refundable security deposit of $2,400. The deposit may be
applied to unpaid rent, unpaid utilities billed under this lease,
cleaning necessary to return the premises to the same level of
cleanliness as at move-in, and repair of damage beyond ordinary wear.

Section 6 - Move-in condition report:

The signed move-in condition report is part of the lease record.
Conditions noted on the move-in report are not chargeable to tenant at
move-out unless tenant caused additional damage beyond the listed
condition.

Section 9 - Walls and fixtures:

Tenant may use small picture nails. Tenant is responsible for unusually
large holes, adhesive damage, unapproved mounting hardware, paint
changes, or wall damage beyond ordinary wear. Routine touch-up painting
after ordinary occupancy is not charged to tenant.

Section 10 - Floor coverings:

Tenant is responsible for stains, burns, tears, pet damage, water
damage caused by tenant negligence, or other floor damage beyond
ordinary wear. Replacement charges should account for age, useful life,
and condition at move-in.

Section 13 - Move-out:

Tenant must remove personal property, remove trash, wipe kitchen and
bathroom surfaces, empty the refrigerator, and return keys to the
leasing office. Landlord may charge for additional cleaning needed to
return the premises to the move-in level of cleanliness.
