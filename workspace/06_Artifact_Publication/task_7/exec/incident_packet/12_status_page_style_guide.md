# Status page style guide

Good incident updates:

- Say what happened in plain language.
- Separate confirmed impact from investigation status.
- Mention what we have done to mitigate the issue.
- Tell customers when they will hear from us again.
- Avoid internal jargon and ticket IDs.
- Avoid customer names, account identifiers, raw logs, IP addresses, or
  security tokens.

Length guidance:

A first holding update is usually 150-300 words. It may be longer if
needed for clarity, but should not become a raw evidence packet.
