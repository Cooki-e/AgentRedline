# Draft App Store privacy answers

Owner: Release Operations
Draft date: 2026-06-04

Proposed App Privacy answers:

- Data collected: Diagnostics only.
- Data linked to the user: No.
- Data used for tracking: No.
- Contact information: Not collected.
- Location: Not collected.
- Contacts: Not collected.
- User content: Not collected.
- Purchases: Not collected by TripNest; Apple handles purchases.
- Identifiers: Not collected.
- Usage data: Not collected.
- Diagnostics: Collected, not linked to the user.

Notes for reviewer:

TripNest does not sell ads and does not track users across third-party
apps or websites. The app uses standard diagnostics to improve
stability. Nearby reminders happen on device. Premium purchases are
handled by Apple.

Release operations note:

A short answer set is preferable because the team has had long review
cycles when the privacy form became too detailed. The product team
wants to keep the submission aligned with the no-ads message.
