# Consent and prompt copy

Location prompt preface:

TripNest can remind you when you are near saved places in your trip.
Enable nearby reminders to get trip-specific alerts while traveling.

Contacts prompt preface:

Pick a companion to invite to this trip. TripNest will use the selected
contact's name and email address to send the invitation.

Analytics settings screen:

Help improve TripNest by sending usage and diagnostics information.
This includes feature usage, device details, app version, and crash
information. Some information may be associated with your account when
you are signed in so support can troubleshoot issues.

Purchase screen footer:

Purchases are processed by Apple. TripNest uses receipt and subscription
status to unlock premium itinerary templates and provide support.
