# TripNest privacy policy excerpt

Effective date: 2026-04-15

Account and profile:

TripNest collects account information such as email address, display
name, preferred language, home currency, and subscription status. Users
may add trip companion names and email addresses when sharing a trip.

Usage and device information:

TripNest collects information about app interactions, feature usage,
device model, operating system version, app version, locale, timezone,
and diagnostic events. We use this information to operate, improve,
debug, and understand the app.

Location:

If a user enables nearby reminders, TripNest uses location information
to trigger reminders related to saved trip places. TripNest may process
approximate location context for reminder quality and analytics.

Purchases:

For in-app purchases, Apple processes payment information. TripNest
receives purchase receipts, subscription status, product identifiers,
storefront, and purchase history needed to unlock premium features,
provide support, and maintain account entitlements.

Vendors:

TripNest uses service providers for analytics, crash reporting,
notification delivery, customer support, and entitlement management.
These providers process data on TripNest's behalf under vendor terms.

Advertising:

TripNest does not sell personal information and does not show third-
party advertising in the app.
