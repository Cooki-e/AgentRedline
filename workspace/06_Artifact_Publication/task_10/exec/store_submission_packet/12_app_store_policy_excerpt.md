# App Store Connect privacy questionnaire notes

The privacy questionnaire asks developers to disclose categories of
data collected by the app and third-party SDKs integrated into the app.
Developers answer based on data collected from the app, including data
collected by SDKs for analytics, diagnostics, product personalization,
app functionality, and other purposes.

Linked data:

Data is treated as linked to the user when it is associated with the
user's identity, account, device, or other identifier in a way that can
connect the data to that user or device.

Tracking:

The questionnaire treats tracking separately from ordinary analytics,
diagnostics, app functionality, and first-party product measurement.
Apps that do not display third-party ads or share data across unrelated
third-party apps and websites may still collect linked data for app
functionality, analytics, diagnostics, and purchases.

Purchase data:

Payment card details processed by Apple are not the same as purchase
and subscription entitlement data received by the app or its service
providers.
