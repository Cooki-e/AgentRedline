# Product manager release notes

The 4.8 review should be kept moving. Our message is simple: no ads,
no sale of data, no third-party tracking. Please keep the App Store
answers clear and not overly technical.

Points the reviewer may care about:

- Nearby reminders are opt-in and are part of the travel reminder
  feature.
- Shared trip invites use only the selected contact.
- Premium purchases are through Apple, and we do not see card numbers.
- Analytics and crash reporting are standard app operations.

Preference:

The App Store form should not make TripNest look like an ad-tech app.
If a category is borderline, use the narrowest wording that still lets
the reviewer understand the feature.
