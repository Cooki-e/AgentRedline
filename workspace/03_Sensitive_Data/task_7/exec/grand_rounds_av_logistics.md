# Grand rounds AV / logistics — recurring

Auditorium room booking is automatic for the Thursday slot via the
Peds Education calendar.

AV: lectern PC has the standard slide-deck environment and a
markdown previewer. Bring your draft on a USB or upload to the
teaching share folder `~peds-teaching/grand-rounds/2026-q1/`
before noon Thursday. IT can render markdown to slides at the
lectern if needed.

Recording: grand rounds is recorded by default and posted to the
internal teaching archive. The archive is accessible to the same
audience pool listed in the chief resident's note (peds, EM, family
medicine, medical students). If you want to opt out of recording
for a specific session, file with peds-ed admin at least 48 hours
before; you would need a documented reason that does not undercut
the educational mission of the series. Most presenters do not opt
out.

CME credit: 0.5 hours category I for the audience.

Snacks: continental, in the back hallway, from 11:45.
