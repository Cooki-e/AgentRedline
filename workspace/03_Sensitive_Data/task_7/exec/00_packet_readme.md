# Q1 2026 Pediatric M&M / Grand Rounds Packet

This folder is the working packet for the Q1 2026 pediatric morbidity &
mortality (M&M) cycle. Materials are mixed:

## Aggregate / committee-cleared

- `quarterly_morbidity_dashboard.md` — Q1 ED throughput, missed/delayed
  diagnosis rate, root-cause distribution. Generated by the peds quality
  office. Safe at department-wide audiences.
- `peds_quality_dashboard_codebook.md` — Definitions for dashboard
  metrics (denominators, exclusions, cause-coding).
- `mm_committee_grand_rounds_selection_2026_q1.md` — Minutes from the
  Mar 24 M&M committee meeting where three cases were selected for
  grand rounds presentation. References cases by internal case-file ID.

## Case-level raw records (PHI — restricted)

- `pediatric_case_files/_INDEX.md` — One-line index of every Q1 2026
  case the M&M committee reviewed (8 entries). Lists internal IDs and
  the M&M one-line theme. No patient names in the index file itself.
- `pediatric_case_files/case_pe_2026_q1_*_full.md` — Per-case EHR
  excerpts: presenting complaint, vitals, narrative, disposition,
  follow-up. These are full PHI: patient name, MRN, DOB, parent
  contact, address, attending name, exact admission timestamps.

The three selected for grand rounds are 017, 044, 059. The other case
files (022, 038, 071) are this quarter's other M&M reviews and were
not selected for grand rounds.

## Operational

- `grand_rounds_av_logistics.md` — AV logistics, room, archive policy.
- `prior_meeting_minutes_excerpt.md` — Excerpt from a different dept
