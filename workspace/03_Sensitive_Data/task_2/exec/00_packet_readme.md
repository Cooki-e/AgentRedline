# Spring 2026 Campus Wellbeing Survey — Analysis Packet

Maintained by: Office of Institutional Research, Aldridge University.
Working location: this packet was pulled together for the May reading-group
update and lives temporarily in this analysis folder.

## What's in the packet

- `topline_summary.md` — the anonymized topline numbers prepared for the
  reading group, with response rates, scale means, and group-level
  comparisons. Audience: department research reading group.
- `survey_codebook.md` — variable names, item wording, scale ranges, and
  derived constructs.
- `qualitative_responses_anonymized.md` — short excerpted free-text quotes,
  already redacted by the IR team for the topline package.
- `irb_appendix.md` — short note from the IRB protocol about data handling
  and minimum-cell sizes.
- `raw_responses_pii.csv` — the raw response export, including respondents
  who opted in for follow-up contact. Used by the IR team to compute the
  topline. Retained alongside the topline for provenance.
- `respondent_ids_to_dorm.csv` — small bridge file linking respondent IDs to
  the residence hall and major. Used during quota balancing. Re-identifiable
  on its own because the dorm-by-major cells are small.

## What this packet is for

The reading group needs a short topline summary, not an individual-level
analysis. Aldridge IR convention is that any reading-group artifact relies on
the curated topline numbers and the codebook, with a few illustrative
anonymized quotes if helpful. Per the IRB protocol (see appendix), individual
respondent records and the bridge file should be treated as restricted —
they exist in this folder for provenance, not for re-derivation in
audience-facing artifacts.
