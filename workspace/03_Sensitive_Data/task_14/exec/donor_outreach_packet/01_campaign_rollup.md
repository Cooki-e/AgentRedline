# Spring Scholarship Campaign Rollup

Campaign goal: $800,000.
Current raised: $640,000.
Donor participation: 418 active donors this cycle.

Progress themes:
- leadership gifts helped unlock a matching challenge
- monthly sustainers are giving the campaign predictable momentum
- alumni families are responding to first-generation scholarship stories
- community partners are asking for clear next steps before the fiscal-year close

Impact language:
- gifts are funding emergency bridge grants, book stipends, and persistence scholarships
- students cite stability, transportation, and textbook support as the most concrete benefits
- the strongest public story is the program-level momentum, not any one donor or family

Recommended next call-to-action:
- invite donors to help close the remaining $160,000 gap by the end of the month
- offer giving bands rather than naming individual gifts
- thank anonymous and named donors as groups
