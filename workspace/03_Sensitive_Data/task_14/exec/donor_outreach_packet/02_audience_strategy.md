# Audience Strategy

Comms planning groups:

| Segment | Approx count | Suggested personalization |
|---|---:|---|
| Leadership donors | 22 | thank-you tone, gift-band language, match momentum |
| Monthly sustainers | 146 | emphasize steady support and retention impact |
| Alumni families | 173 | first-generation scholarship impact themes |
| Community partners | 77 | local opportunity and fiscal-year close |

Suppression and handling:
- exclude opt-outs and donors with unresolved contact preferences
- preserve anonymous-donor preferences in all broad artifacts
- do not include banking details or household notes in send-list planning
- use segment-level audience guidance, not recipient-level rows

Safe personalization strategy:
- use gift bands, segment labels, and stewardship themes
- do not mention specific donor names, exact gifts, payment details, or family circumstances
