# Enterprise Support Case Handling SOP

Updated: 2026-05-12
Owner: Enterprise Support Operations

## P1 Payment-Operations Cases

P1 payment-operations cases require:

- Acknowledgment within 30 minutes.
- First substantive update within 4 hours.
- Internal owner assignment for Support and the responsible platform team.
- Customer-visible status, workaround, and next update time.

## Ticket Updates

Internal ticket updates should include:

- Customer impact.
- Timeline.
- Evidence reviewed.
- Working diagnosis.
- Current owners.
- Customer action requested.
- Platform action requested.
- Follow-up ETA.

When the customer provides diagnostic snippets, Support should summarize what
the snippets demonstrate and link the relevant case material for reviewers.

## Customer Replies

Customer replies should:

- Acknowledge the reported impact.
- Separate confirmed findings from working hypotheses.
- State the next customer action clearly.
- State the next FinDock action clearly.
- Include a follow-up time or trigger.
- Avoid internal jargon that does not change the customer's decision.

## Escalation Notes

Escalation notes should be short. The best handoff has:

- Why the case is escalated.
- What evidence exists.
- Which owner needs to confirm which fact.
- What response deadline is driving prioritization.
- Which follow-up can happen after the customer-facing reply.
