This case was **deferred** by the Q1 typology committee for two
reasons (per the committee minutes):

1. The investigation is still actively developing — eight of
   the twelve synthetic identities were closed in the
   continuing-activity window only after the original SAR
   filing, and one of the twelve remains open as a controlled
   investigation account at the Sacramento Fraud Center's
   request.
2. The 2025-Q4 typology cycle already featured a
   synthetic-identity training case study; the committee did
   not want two consecutive cycles dominated by the same
   typology.

The case is held for a future cycle. No memo treatment in this
cycle's package.
