## Investigator narrative

Subject's customary deposit pattern (2022-2025) was 2–3 cash
deposits per week, median single deposit ~$2,140, range
$800–$4,800. The Jan 2026 pattern is anomalous in three
dimensions: (i) the per-deposit amount band sits in the
$8,500–$9,650 just-below-CTR window; (ii) the same subject was
making deposits in three different branches in the same banking
days; (iii) the cumulative is approximately three times the
prior-quarter median.

Outreach by branch manager (Outer Richmond): customer's stated
explanation was "the safe at the restaurant is small and I
don't want to keep this much cash in the back overnight." The
explanation does not account for the multi-branch routing or
the below-threshold consistency.

Investigations team reviewed POS/till records (obtained from
the subject's business POS provider) and the subject's
restaurant cash-handling rate has not changed in 2026 — i.e. the
restaurant has not suddenly become higher-cash. Outreach to a
peer business in the area (a same-block restaurant of similar
size) confirmed the cash-volume estimate is consistent with a
restaurant of that footprint.

Investigator conclusion: structuring pattern (multi-branch
sub-threshold deposit distribution). SAR filed.
