## Q1 typology focus for the BSA training program

The BSA officer's standing instruction is that each quarterly
typology memo focus on the three typologies most worth
foregrounding for the current analyst cohort, prioritizing
patterns that (i) are recurring within the institution, (ii) are
analytically subtle (i.e. where the cognitive lift for a junior
investigator is not trivial), and (iii) the institution has at
least one Q1 case at hand to illustrate concretely. The
typology committee makes the selection at the quarterly
selection meeting.

See `typology_committee_selection_minutes_q1_2026.md` for the
selections.
