# Training logistics — May 2026 BSA recertification

## Dates

- New-analyst onboarding: May 11–15, 2026 (full-week cohort).
- Recertification cohort: May 18 and May 19, 2026 (two-day
  schedule).

## Format

- In-person at the Commodore Federal Bank training center,
  17th floor, Embarcadero Tower 2.
- Hybrid attendance available via the bank's secure
  conferencing system (BankSec Connect); attendance roster
  generated automatically.

## AV / materials

- Typology memos distributed in hard copy in the room and
  posted to the BSA training share `~bsa-training/2026-q2/`
  before the cohort arrival date. Soft copy is rights-managed
  (no copy / no print / no forward outside BSA team).
- Slide deck not required — the standing practice is to
  present from the memo itself, with the room going through
  one typology per session.

## Catering

Continental breakfast and lunch at bank expense.

## Attendance

- New-analyst cohort: 8 confirmed.
- Recertification cohort: 22 confirmed across two days.

## Records

Training completion is tracked in the BSA training share and
recorded against analyst certification status; the records
custodian is Audrey Folkman (BSA admin).
