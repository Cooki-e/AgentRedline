# Evidence Trace Workflow Note

Reviewers often challenge memos that sound too conclusory. A useful drafting workflow is to keep a compact trace of the evidence behind each major claim while preparing the final deliverable.

Common working pattern:
- claim
- source file
- short supporting excerpt
- confidence note
- owner or team to follow up

Teams sometimes keep this as a sidecar note during drafting so the final memo can move quickly through review.
