# Credit Risk Committee Brief — March 2026 (Prior Month)

Snapshot: 2026-03-31. Audience: Credit Risk Committee.

This is the prior-month committee brief. Use it as the model for shape,
voice, and the evidence section.

---

## Headline

Portfolio 30+ DPD rose 30bps month-over-month to 5.1%; 90+ DPD rose
20bps to 2.3%. Movement is concentrated in the 2024-H1 origination
cohort and the FHA product. Roll-rate from Current to 30+ moved from
1.1% to 1.2%. Net charge-off rate annualized at 0.62% (vs 0.58%
prior month).

## Cohort drivers

- **2024-H1 vintage** continues to underperform expectations. 30+ DPD
  at 8.9% (prior month 8.5%), 220bps above the rolling vintage mean.
  Sub-680 FICO concentration; Southeast / Sun Belt MSA concentration.
- **FHA product** 30+ DPD at 7.6% (prior month 7.0%). 2023-H2 and
  2024-H1 FHA cohorts contribute most of the increase.
- **ARM 7/1 reset population** (2018-Q1 originations resetting in
  February) is at 8.2% 30+ DPD in its first post-reset month.
- **Florida property-event** category continues at elevated share
  driven by insurance non-renewal carryover.

## Root-cause signal (March)

Employment shock 27.3% of RPC volume (+2.1pp), medical shock 20.0%
(+1.4pp), property event 11.2% (+1.8pp), ARM rate shock 5.3% (first
month of reset wave appearance).

## Recommended posture

- **Capital reserve**: maintain current reserve level for 2024-H1
  conforming; revisit after April snapshot if 30+ DPD trend
  continues.
- **Pricing**: tighten 2024-H1-like profile pricing by 25bps for new
  originations in sub-680 FICO + Southeast / Sun Belt geographies.
- **Underwriting**: no immediate change; revisit if 2024-H2 vintage
  performance worsens.
- **Loss Mitigation capacity**: confirmed up-staffing for April
  through Q3 to absorb expected ARM-reset RPC volume.
