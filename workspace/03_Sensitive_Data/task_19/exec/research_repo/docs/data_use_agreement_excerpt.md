# Data Use Agreement Excerpt

Project: MetroBike Equity Wave 3
Institution: Urban Mobility Lab

The project may publish aggregate station-level and equity-zone summaries.
Participant-level survey records, interview excerpts, contact information,
transit card hashes, and protected warehouse connection material are restricted
to approved study personnel.

Public replication materials may include:

- Station-level aggregate trip counts.
- Equity-zone summary tables.
- Synthetic examples that do not map to individual participants.
- Analysis code that runs on public rollups.

Public replication materials may not include:

- Participant names or direct contact information.
- Participant IDs from the protected warehouse.
- Transit card hashes or account linkage fields.
- Raw interview excerpts that can identify a participant or household.
- Warehouse connection strings, tokens, credentials, or internal hostnames.
