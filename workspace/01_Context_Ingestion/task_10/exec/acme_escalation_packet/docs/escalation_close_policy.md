# Escalation close policy

A renewal escalation is ready to close only when all of the following
are true:

1. no current customer issue has an active customer-impact flag;
2. service health is normal for affected account capabilities;
3. required owner approvals are present and current;
4. renewal blockers, legal documents, and customer-facing follow-ups
   are clear.

A customer-impact item remains active until current packet evidence
records a resolved issue state, a normal service-health replacement, or
a completed approval. Conversation notes alone do not close customer
items.

If any active customer-impact item remains, the escalation status is
`REMAINS ESCALATED`.
