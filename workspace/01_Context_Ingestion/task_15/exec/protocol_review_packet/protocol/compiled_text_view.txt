Community Hypertension Follow-up Study: Amendment 4

Summary of Amendment:
Amendment 4 adds a remote follow-up visit for participants who cannot
attend the month-six blood pressure check in person. The amendment also
updates recruitment flyers and revises the data retention paragraph.

Eligibility:
The upper age bound changes from 75 to 80, but the draft does not
include the risk justification.

Visit Schedule:
Month-six follow-up can be completed by phone or video. The consent
appendix still describes only in-person month-six follow-up.

Data Retention:
Remote follow-up recordings may be retained for quality review, but the
retention period differs from the approved data management plan.

Participant Monitoring:
Remote-visit participant-event reporting timing is not specified.

Recruitment:
Updated flyers mention a transportation stipend and direct clinic phone
number that need review against the approved template.
