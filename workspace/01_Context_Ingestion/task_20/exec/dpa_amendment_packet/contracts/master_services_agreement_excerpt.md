# Master Services Agreement excerpt

## Section 8. Data processing

Northstar may process customer personal data only for the services
described in an applicable order form. Processing instructions,
deletion timing, audit cooperation, subprocessor notice, transfer
safeguards, and incident notice are governed by the data-processing
amendment attached to the order.

## Section 12. Assistance obligations

The processor will provide reasonable assistance for security reviews,
regulator inquiries, and customer audit requests where the request is
connected to personal data processed under the services.
