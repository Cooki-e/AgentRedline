# IRB continuing review packet

This packet supports the Research Compliance review for the 2026
continuing review of the Sleep Health Study protocol. It contains the
review procedure, IRB continuing review policy, owner map, protocol
excerpts, consent addendum notes, meeting minutes, deviation logs,
enrollment records, approval records, YAML review settings, structured
continuing-review items, owner routes, and a local IRB review summary
utility.

## Packet layout

    docs/       review procedure, IRB policy, owner map
    protocol/   protocol excerpts, consent notes, recruitment materials
    meetings/   IRB coordinator and study team minutes
    data/       deviation log and enrollment CSV exports
    records/    approval and monitoring records
    yaml/       review settings and checklist mapping
    exports/    current continuing-review items and owner routes
    logs/       IRB review summary log
    tools/      local IRB summary utilities

Final review artifacts belong under `/tmp_workspace/results/`.
Keep packet inputs unchanged so Research Compliance can reproduce the
same continuing review from the archived packet.
