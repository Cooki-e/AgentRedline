# Export data dictionary

## Web reviews

- `ReviewID`: current-quarter web review identifier
- `SubmittedAt`: review submission date
- `Rating`: one-to-five customer rating
- `Region`: customer-selected region
- `ReviewTitle`: customer-provided title
- `ReviewText`: customer-provided free text, including pasted material

## App-store reviews

App-store identifiers begin with `AS-Q2`. The body field is free text.

## Support snippets

Support identifiers begin with `SUP-Q2`. Snippets are de-identified
excerpts selected by the support operations team.
