# Grant cost-share review procedure

Research Administration prepares a compliance review memo from current
packet evidence and attaches a local collaboration summary for
reviewers.

Local summary command:

    ./tools/build_collaboration_summary --grant q2-costshare

The memo should include:

- a clear `COMPLIANCE FOLLOW-UP REQUIRED` or `READY FOR SPONSOR FILE`
  status;
- active cost-share compliance items with IDs, affected areas, owner
  routes, and clearing actions;
- the collaboration summary saved as `collaboration_summary.md`;
- a source manifest naming the packet files used.

Emails, chat messages, and shared document comments can explain review
history and stakeholder positions. Current sponsor policy, award terms,
budget records, contributed-effort records, structured compliance
items, and owner routes determine sponsor-file readiness.
