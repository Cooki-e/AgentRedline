# Award terms excerpt

Award: GRT-2026-0421
Sponsor: North Coast Science Foundation
Period: 2026 Q2 closeout

The recipient must maintain verifiable documentation for all committed
cost-share contributions. Cash cost-share must reconcile to the
approved budget. Contributed effort must be certified by the principal
investigator. Third-party in-kind contributions must include valuation
support. Equipment match must include purchase evidence. Subaward
partner certification must be retained in the sponsor file.
