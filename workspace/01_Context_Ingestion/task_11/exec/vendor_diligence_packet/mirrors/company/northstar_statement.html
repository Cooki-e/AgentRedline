Statement on component shipment documentation

2026-05-25

Northstar Robotics stated that it is cooperating with officials during a routine documentation review for a component shipment. The company said the goods were manufactured by a long-standing contract supplier and that supplemental origin records would be provided.

The statement does not address the beneficial ownership mismatch in the corporate registry extract, the missing end-use questionnaire appendix, or the expired insurance certificate in the procurement file.
