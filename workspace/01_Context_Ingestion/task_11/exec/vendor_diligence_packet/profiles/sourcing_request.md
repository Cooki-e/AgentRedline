# Sourcing request summary

Strategic Sourcing requested a diligence brief for Northstar Robotics
because the warehouse automation pilot needs a shortlisted supplier
for conveyor sensors, scanner stands, and maintenance support.

The review does not approve a purchase order. It determines whether
Northstar Robotics can move into sourcing review while Compliance,
Trade Compliance, Vendor Management, and Procurement Operations track
any remaining diligence items.

Requested commercial scope:

- pilot hardware kits for two regional distribution centers;
- installation support during the August site-readiness window;
- warranty and service coverage for one year after acceptance;
- optional extension for additional facilities after pilot review.
