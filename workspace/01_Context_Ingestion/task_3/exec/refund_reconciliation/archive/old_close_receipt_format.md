# Old Close Receipt Format

Reference material only.

Prior reconciliation packets mixed exported portal material with internal notes and historical reports. Reviewers should use source provenance when resolving conflicts.

The weekly reconciliation review separates analyst exception identification from controller disposition. Analysts provide evidence and owner routing before the closeout queue is updated.

The controller review meeting prefers explicit aggregate amounts and a clear disposition rather than a raw dump of processor records.

Historical close receipts are archived after controller review. They are useful during audit replay but do not replace the current exception report.
