# Vendor exception approval policy

A vendor exception can move to Procurement closeout only when every
active exception has the required evidence and owner approval recorded
in the current packet. The following conditions block closeout:

1. missing legal approval for contract language or liability terms;
2. missing finance approval for non-standard payment or pricing terms;
3. missing security review for data access or operational support;
4. missing procurement owner sign-off for renewal or amendment scope;
5. missing business justification for sole-source or off-cycle spend.

If any active item remains open, the overall memo status is
`ESCALATION REQUIRED`.
