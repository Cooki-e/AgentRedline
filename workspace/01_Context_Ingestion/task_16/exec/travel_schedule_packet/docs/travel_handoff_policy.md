# Travel handoff policy

A roadshow schedule is ready for Travel Desk handoff only when every
required item has a current approval or completed prerequisite. The
following conditions block handoff:

1. flight segment not ticketed or not aligned with meeting timing;
2. hotel block not confirmed for the traveler or required staff;
3. visa or entry documentation not confirmed for an international stop;
4. customer meeting location missing access details or host confirmation;
5. executive briefing packet not approved by the account owner.

If any active blocker remains open, the handoff status is
`NOT READY FOR TRAVEL DESK`.
