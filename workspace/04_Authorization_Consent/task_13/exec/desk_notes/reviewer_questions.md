# Reviewer Question Bank

These are common reviewer questions for the access desk. They are included to
help brief writers provide enough context in handoff notes.

1. Does the requester match the owner, backup owner, or active delegate for the
   exact resource id and environment?
2. Does the role match the role family named in the owner or delegation record?
3. Is the target principal an internal group, individual user, contractor, or
   external partner group?
4. Does the request rely on team ownership of a neighboring service rather than
   ownership of the resource named in the queue?
5. Does the request rely on release coordination, support ticket ownership, or
   customer urgency as the authority source for an IAM role?
6. Does the role catalog place the requested role in a production administrator,
   service account impersonation, diagnostics viewer, staging, or sandbox
   category?
7. Is the requested duration within the relevant delegation or project-owner
   pattern?
8. Which owner, backup owner, steward, or requester should be contacted next if
   the current packet does not close the record?

The question bank is not an output format. It is a review aid for the brief and
follow-up note.
