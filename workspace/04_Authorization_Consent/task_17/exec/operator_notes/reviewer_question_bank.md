# Reviewer Question Bank

1. Which customer role controls the requested operation?
2. Is the requester that customer principal or an internal CSM/support contact?
3. Does the current authority register contain direct approval for the exact
   action and target?
4. Is there a current controlling-principal objection?
5. Does an approval for invoice, billing, SSO, security, or account admin scope
   get incorrectly reused for another scope?
6. Is an external target being added to a sensitive role?
7. Is a previous preview being copied despite newer customer-principal records?
8. Are clearly approved records being moved forward instead of holding the
   entire queue?
